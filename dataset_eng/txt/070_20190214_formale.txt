The data model describes a shipment management system. The entities `Category` (PK: CCD), `Destination` (PK: CDes), `Type` (PK: CT) and `Dimension` (PK: CDim) are master data tables, each with a unique identifier code and a `Description` attribute. The `Sender` entity is identified by a `CM` code and has a `Name` attribute. A one-to-many relationship is established between `Category` and `Destination`. The central entity `Shipment` is identified by a unique `ID` and serves as a convergence point for several one-to-many relationships: each shipment is associated with a single `Destination`, a single `Type` and a single `Sender`. The `Parcel` entity is dependent on the `Shipment` entity through a one-to-many identifying relationship; its primary key is in fact composite, made up of a parcel number `NP` and the foreign key `fk_shipping` which refers to the shipment it belongs to. Each `Package` is also associated with a single `Dimension`.
