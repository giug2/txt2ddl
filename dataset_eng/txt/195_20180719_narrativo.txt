This system was designed to efficiently and detailedly manage the entire supply and logistics chain, tracking each product from its supplier to its exact physical location in the warehouse. The organization starts from a solid geographical base: everything originates from a country, uniquely identified by an acronym and its full name. Each country can host numerous Cities, each with its own identifier and name, but each city belongs unambiguously to only one Country, creating a clear territorial hierarchy. It is within these cities that our Warehouses are located, the beating heart of operations. Each warehouse, with its specific name and identification code, is located in one and only one city, although a city can naturally host more than one warehouse.

Within each warehouse, the organization becomes even more granular. The space is divided into Shelves, each defined by a unique code within its own warehouse and by a maximum capacity. A shelf cannot exist except within a warehouse, and a warehouse is made up of a multitude of shelves. Moving on to the goods we manage, each Product is meticulously cataloged with an SKU code that unambiguously identifies it, a descriptive name and its year of production. The origin of these products is fundamental: each product is supplied by a single supplier. Suppliers are registered with their company name, VAT number and an identification code, and each of them is based in a specific country. A single supplier may supply us with many different products, but each product in our inventory has a clear and unique origin. Finally, the system links the product to its physical location: a shelf can contain many different products, but each type of product, identified by its SKU, is assigned to a single and specific shelf, thus ensuring impeccable organization and rapid localization of any item.
