This system was designed to dynamically and transparently manage the beating heart of every organization: the people and the initiatives they carry out. At the center of our story we find two fundamental protagonists. On the one hand we have the Employee, each unique individual, identified unequivocally by his Serial Number. For each employee, we store essential information such as the Surname and First Name, as well as a significant date in their professional career, the Hiring Date, which marks the beginning of their adventure with us. On the other hand, there are the Projects, which represent the objectives and challenges that the company aims to achieve. Each Project is clearly distinguished from the others thanks to a unique Project Code, which guarantees perfect traceability. The real magic of this system, however, manifests itself in the way these two worlds meet and collaborate. A single employee can put their skills to good use by participating in multiple projects at the same time, and, similarly, each project is the result of the joint effort of a team made up of multiple employees. This flexibility is crucial, but to get a complete picture it's not enough to know who works on what; it's critical to understand *how* it contributes. For this reason, every time an employee is assigned to a project, their Role in that specific context is also specified. In this way, a person could be a 'Project Manager' on one initiative and a 'Functional Analyst' on another, offering a clear and detailed vision of the organization of work and the valorisation of talents.
