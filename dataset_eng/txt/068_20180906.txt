The system described manages the personal information of people by storing for each person the tax code, surname, name and date of birth 
Each person can own one or more telephone accounts which are uniquely identified by the prefix and number attribute pair and are associated with a specific address 
There is a direct link between a user and its owner since the Owner field must refer to a valid tax code present in the person registry 
The geographical component is structured on multiple levels, each user belongs to a district identified by its prefix 
The districts are in turn characterized by a name and the province they belong to 
This province connected by its acronym to an entity that describes the Italian provinces, each defined by its own acronym and full name 
For each user, bills are periodically issued, each identified by a unique code and containing the date of issue and the amount 
Each bill unequivocally associated with a single user through the prefix and number pair, thus ensuring that each invoice is correctly linked to the service to which it refers 
Finally, the system tracks the payments made to settle bills 
Each payment recorded with its own code, date and amount and strictly linked to a specific existing bill ensuring the integrity and traceability of financial transactions
