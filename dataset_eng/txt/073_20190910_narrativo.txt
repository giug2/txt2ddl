This system was designed to efficiently and organizedly manage the professional activities carried out on behalf of people who need assistance. At the center of our data universe we find the **Patient** entity, which represents each individual customer or user of the service. Each client is uniquely identified by a personal **Code** and is registered with their **Surname** and **Name**, thus creating a clear and precise registry of all the people followed.

For each patient, it is possible to open one or more **Practices**, which function as real digital files. Each case is unique, marked by its own **ID** code and associated with an opening **Date**, allowing you to trace the start of the assistance relationship for a specific case. The structure of the system establishes that while a single client can have multiple cases open over time, each case can always and only be traced back to a single client, thus guaranteeing a direct and unequivocal link between the customer and the work carried out for him.

Each practice comes to life and develops through a series of specific **Performances**, which constitute the operational heart of the system. A service represents every single action, consultancy or service provided within a specific practice. To maintain order and traceability, each service is inextricably linked to its membership practice via a reference (`fk_practice`) and is numbered progressively with a **Line** number, ensuring that each entry is unique within its file. Fundamental details are recorded for each service such as a **Description** of the activity carried out, the **Date** on which it occurred, any associated economic **Amount** and the **Subject** who performed or to whom the intervention refers. This allows you to have a detailed and chronological diary of all the operations carried out, offering a complete and transparent view of the progress of each individual practice.
