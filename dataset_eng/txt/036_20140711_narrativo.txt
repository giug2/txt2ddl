Welcome to the beating heart of our academic organization, a system designed to clearly and precisely map the world of university teaching. The structure is based on solid foundations, the Departments, each uniquely identified by its Name and located at a specific Address. Each Department is the academic "home" of numerous Professors, who represent the intellectual engine of the institution. Each Professor, in turn, is defined by a Name and a unique Code that identifies him, and is strictly linked to a single Department to which he belongs, creating a clear link between person and structure. The core of the training offer is represented by the Courses: each Course is an entity in itself, defined by a unique Code and a Title that describes its content. However, a course comes to life and becomes accessible to students through its annual Editions. Each Edition is a specific instance of a course in a specific academic year, identified by its own Code. This means that a single Course, such as "Mathematical Analysis I", can have multiple Editions over time (one for each year in which it is delivered), while each single Edition refers unequivocally to one and the same Course. The system finds its completeness in the link between who teaches and what is taught at a given moment: each Edition of a course is in fact entrusted to the responsibility of a single Professor. This teacher, despite being able to hold numerous editions of different courses or of the same course in different years, guarantees continuity and teaching quality for that specific offer, thus inextricably linking the teacher, the subject and the academic year.
