This system was designed to tell each customer's financial story in a clear, orderly and complete way. At the center of everything we find the **Current account**, which represents the fulcrum of economic activities. Each account is uniquely identified by a specific **Number** and contains the essential information of its owner, such as the name of the **Owner** and his **Address**. To provide an immediate view of your situation, the system records both the **Initial balance** and the **Final balance**, offering a precise snapshot of its evolution over a given period of time.

The life of a current account is marked by the operations that take place on it. Every single transaction, whether an entry or an exit, is meticulously recorded as a **Movement**. Each movement is a unique event, characterized by its own identifying **Code**, and brings with it crucial information such as the **Date** on which it was performed, a descriptive **Detail** and, of course, the **Amount** that changed the balance. The link between these two elements is fundamental: each **Current Account** is the home of a multitude of **Transactions**, which together make up the detailed account statement, while each single movement indisputably belongs to one and the same account.

To make this financial history even more readable and easy to analyze, each transaction is classified. For this purpose there is the **Movement Type**, a simple but powerful category identified by an **Id** and a clear **Description**, such as "Incoming bank transfer", "ATM withdrawal" or "Utility payment". Each **Movement** registered in the system must be associated with a precise **Movement Type**, thus allowing you to easily group, filter and understand the nature of your expenses and earnings. While a single category, such as "Salary Credit", may be common to countless transactions, each specific transaction falls into one and only one of these types, thus ensuring order and clarity in financial management.
