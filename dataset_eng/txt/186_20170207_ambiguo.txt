So, basically here we have a few things that revolve around the university. There are the teachers, each with their own identification code so as not to confuse them. Then there are the Degree Courses, which also have their own unique number to understand which one we are talking about. The matter becomes interesting with the Students: to know exactly who a student is, his/her student number is not enough, you also have to know which Degree Course he/she is enrolled in, it's as if his/her identity were a piece here and a piece there. Of course, a lot of students can be in the same course, but one student is only in that one. Finally, there are the Exams; each exam has its own code, but is always linked to a specific teacher, the one who holds it. A professor can easily manage multiple exams, but an exam always belongs to that professor. The most chaotic part is that obviously each student can take a lot of different exams and, in the same way, each exam is taken by a lot of students. It's a big mix.
