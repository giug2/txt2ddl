This system has been designed to manage the maintenance history of each vehicle in a complete and detailed way, offering a clear and organized picture for workshops and owners. At the center of the system is the Vehicle, a unique entity unequivocally identified by its License Plate. For each vehicle, we not only record essential personal information such as the Owner's name and Address, but we also keep track of its life on the road, storing both the Initial Mileage at the time of first registration and the Current Mileage, which is constantly updated. The real strength of the system lies in its ability to build a precise history of all the operations carried out. In fact, every single Vehicle is connected to a series of Interventions, thus creating a complete historical register. Each Service is an event in its own right, with its own unique Service Code, the Date it was performed, the associated Cost and a space for detailed Notes where mechanics can describe the work performed. To ensure order and ease of analysis, each Intervention is not a generic action, but is classified according to a precise typology. In fact, there is a catalog of Types of Intervention, where each type is defined by a Type Code and a clear Description (for example, "oil change", "brake pad replacement" or "full service"). In this way, a single type of intervention can be associated with countless specific interventions carried out on different vehicles, allowing the frequency and nature of the work carried out by the workshop to be easily analysed.
