The data model defines seven entities. The `CasaEditrice` entity is identified by a `Publisher Code` (PK) and has a `Name`. The `Language` entity is defined by `LanguageCode` (PK) and `Name`. The `Genre` entity is identified by `GenreCode` (PK) and has a `Name`. The `User` entity is identified by `UserCode` (PK) and includes the `LastName` and `FirstName` attributes. The `Collana` entity is identified by a composite primary key made up of `NomeCollana` and `fk_editore`, which acts as a foreign key towards `CasaEditrice`. The `Edition` entity has a composite primary key made up of `EditionCode` and `fk_collana` (foreign key to `Collana`) and contains the `NumeroPagine` attribute. Finally, the `PhysicalCopy` entity is uniquely identified by `InventoryCode` (PK) and described by `AnnoStampa`. The relationships between the entities are as follows: a one-to-many (1:N) relationship links `Lingua` to `CasaEditrice`, `CasaEditrice` to `Collana`, `Genre` to `Collana`, `Collana` to `Edizione` and `Edizione` to `CopiaFisica`. A many-to-many (N:N) relationship is also defined between `User` and `PhysicalCopy`.
