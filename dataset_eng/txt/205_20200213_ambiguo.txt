So, basically there is this stuff that we call Company, which has its own unique code to recognize it and that's it. Then, every company is made up of a few Divisions. The interesting thing is that to identify a Division its number is not enough, you also need to know which company it is linked to, so both information is needed. In short, a company can have a lot of divisions, but a division belongs only to one company. Inside each division, then, you will find the people who work there, the Employees. Here too the matter is similar: to know who an employee is, you need his personal code and also the division in which he is located, otherwise nothing can be understood. Obviously, a lot of employees can all fit in the same division. Finally, there are the Projects. Each project is managed by a division and, as with employees, to identify it you need its code and the reference division. In practice, a division can have many projects on the line, but a specific project is always the stuff of only one division.
