So basically here we keep track of a few people and their money in the bank. On the one hand we have the list of customers, and for each one we save the usual things: name, surname, where they live, date of birth and, fundamentally, their tax code which is the only data that cannot be the same for two different people. On the other side there are the current accounts, each with its own identification number, which is unique for each account. This is how it works: each customer can have a lot of current accounts in their belly, there is no limit, but every single current account is necessarily registered in the name of a single person, there is no escape.
