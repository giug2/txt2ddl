Welcome to the beating heart of our system, a digital universe designed to orchestrate the magic and logistics of major musical events. Imagine being able to follow an entire sonic journey, from its conception to the last applause: this is what our data structure allows you to do. At the pinnacle of it all we find the **Tour**, the epic musical adventure that unites a series of performances under one name. Each tour is absolutely unique, identified by its own `Tour Code` and known by the public thanks to its evocative `Tour Name`.

A tour, of course, is not an abstract entity, but comes to life through its **Concerts**. Every single concert is a stage of this great journey, an unrepeatable event inextricably linked to the tour of which it is part. For this reason, each concert is defined not only by its specific `Concert Code`, but also by the code of the tour to which it belongs, as well as by its precise `Date` in the calendar. Every performance needs a place to happen, and in fact every concert takes place in one and only one **City**, a well-defined location with its own `CityCode` and name.

Going into even more detail, each concert is a complex evening, a mosaic of unforgettable moments. For this reason, we break it down into individual **Performances**. An evening might include an opening band, the artist's main set, and perhaps an acoustic encore; each of these moments is a performance in itself, with its own `PerformanceCode`, a precise `StartTime` and a `SetDescription` that tells its content.

But who are the protagonists on stage? This is where our flexible artist management comes into play. Each performance is animated by one or more participations, registered through the entity **PartecipazioneArtista**. This allows us to manage the most complex situations: a single performance could see a duet between two solo artists, or an entire band playing together. Each participation is a unique event, with its own `Participation Code`. This participation connects the performance to the person performing, who can be a solo **Artist**, with his `Artist Code` and his `Art Name`, or a complete **Band**, also identified by a `Band Code` and a `Band Name`. This structure allows us maximum flexibility: an artist can participate in countless performances, and a performance can host multiple artists or bands. To add a touch of history and belonging, each band is also linked to its city of origin, creating a special bond with a specific location. In this way, our system not only organizes events, but tells stories, connecting tours, cities, concerts and talents in a perfectly harmonized symphony of data.
