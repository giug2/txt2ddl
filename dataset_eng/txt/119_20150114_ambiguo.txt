So, in practice the matter is this: there are Regions, each with its own code and name, and within these regions there are scattered a lot of Colleges, which are identified by a simple number. Naturally, each College is confined to a single Region, but a Region can have quite a few Colleges within it. Then we have the Parties, also with a code and a name to recognize them, and the People, each with their own personal code. The crux of it all is when someone is Elected in a certain year. This elected person, who we could call a Deputy, is always linked to a single Party and is elected in a single College. In short, a Party can have a lot of elected representatives around and a Constituency elects its representatives, but a specific Deputy belongs to only one party and to a single constituency for that given election.
