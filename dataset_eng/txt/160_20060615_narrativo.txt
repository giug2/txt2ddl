Welcome to the beating heart of a universe dedicated to live music, a system designed to manage the excitement and competition of large music festivals. At the center of everything is the Festival, each edition identified by a unique code and celebrated with a memorable name. In this great event, the real protagonists are the Bands, each recognized by their own unique name, who come to show off their talent. Naturally, a band is the sum of its components: the Musicians. Each musician is defined by his name and date of birth, but his professional identity is inextricably linked to the band he is part of, through a member ID that makes him unique within his group.

The most awaited moment is the Exhibition, which is not a simple performance, but a real musical battle. On a specific date and on a designated stage, a "headline band" goes head-to-head with a "challenger band", creating a unique and unrepeatable event. The system doesn't just record who plays, it captures every detail: for each performance, we know exactly which musicians took to the stage and what specific role they played in that performance. At the end of each sound duel, the tension is resolved with the assignment of the votes received by each band, a crucial piece of data to determine the outcome of the challenge. As the festival continues, these results accumulate, and eventually, for each participating band, the system calculates an overall total score and determines its final position in the event's overall ranking, thus declaring the winners and writing a new page in the history of music.
