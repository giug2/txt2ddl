A logical scheme for managing museum collections of works of art is presented.
The central entity is OperaArte, described by the attributes OperaID, Title and Style.
The authorship of the works is modeled by a many-to-many relationship, which connects the OperaArte entity to the Artist entity, indicating that a work can be the result of a collaboration between multiple artists and an artist can be the author of multiple works.
The Artist entity is in turn defined by ArtistID, Surname and Name.
Location information is managed by the relationship between OperaArte and Museum, which includes the SalaEspositiva and AnnoAcquisizione attributes.
This relationship establishes a connection between a work and the museum that hosts it, specifying that each work is exhibited in a single museum, while a museum can host many works.
Finally, the Museum entity is characterized by Name and City.
