Show the conceptual scheme of a database for a set of museums, according to the following specifications.
• Each museum has a name, is located in a city (of which the country is also of interest, with its acronym) and
it has a series of rooms, each of which has a name and size.
• Museums exhibit works of art, for each of which the author is interested (with code, surname, name,
date of birth and death), the year of execution and the room in which it is exhibited (which is assumed to be fixed:
each work is located in a room of a museum)
