This system was designed to orchestrate the entire software development universe, transforming ideas into tangible realities through an organized and collaborative workflow. At the center of it all is our `Customer`, identified by a unique `Customer_ID`, a `First Name` and a `Last Name`, which is the source of all innovation. Each customer need is expressed in a `Feature`, a specific software characteristic defined by an `ID_Feature` and a `Name`. It is critical to note that each feature is requested by a single customer, ensuring a clear line of communication and accountability, but can be so versatile that it can be included in more than one `Project`.

Projects are the big initiatives that shape features. Each `Project`, with its `Project_ID`, `Name` and a detailed `Description`, represents a concrete business objective. To ensure flawless execution, each project is targeted to a specific technology `Platform`, such as web or mobile, identified by a `Platform_ID` and a `Name`. Time management is crucial, therefore work on a project is divided into multiple `Sprints`, iterative development cycles, each characterized by a defined `Duration` and a precise `End_Date`, which mark the pace of progress.

The implementation of each project is entrusted to a cohesive development `Team`, identified by its `Name` and located in a specific `Office`. While a team can manage multiple projects at the same time, a single project is always the responsibility of a single team, ensuring consistency and focus. The beating heart of each team is the `Developer`, a professional identified by his `Serial Number`, `Name` and `Surname`.

To orchestrate the work in an even more granular way, the `Assignment` entity comes into play. This powerful tool, identified by an `Assignment_ID`, creates a bridge between people, teams and skills. A single assignment unambiguously links a `Developer` and his `Team` to a precise `Technology` needed for a task. The technologies, in turn, are cataloged with a `Technology_ID`, a `Name` and a `Category`, allowing us to map the skills and needs of the project. This system is so sophisticated that it also allows you to create dependencies: an assignment can in turn depend on others, designing a clear hierarchy of tasks and prerequisites to manage even the most complex workflows. In short, we've created a living map that connects every customer request to a project, a team, specific developers, and the technologies they master, all orchestrated with the precision of detailed sprints and assignments.
