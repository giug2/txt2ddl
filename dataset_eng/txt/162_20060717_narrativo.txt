Imagine a vibrant digital ecosystem designed to manage a complex and dynamic network of restaurant activities, from supply logistics to the culinary offering presented to the customer. At the center of this world we find the Cities, each identified by a unique code and its own name, which act as geographical containers for our operations. Within each individual city, numerous restaurants thrive, each with its own identification code, a distinctive name, a precise address and a telephone number for contacts. At the same time, to guarantee a constant and efficient flow of raw materials, each city can host one or more Logistics Centers, also recognized by a code and a name. These centers are the beating heart of the supply chain and manage complex relationships with a vast network of Suppliers; flexibility is maximum, since a logistics center can collaborate with many different suppliers and, at the same time, a single supplier can serve multiple logistics centers. The identity of each restaurant is expressed through its gastronomic offering: each venue offers a rich assortment of dishes, each defined by a code, a name and a specific price. To make the experience even more attractive, restaurants have the possibility of creating Special Menus, unique offers characterized by a specific period of validity, a fixed price and a pre-established number of courses. Finally, to stimulate the market, each restaurant can join and launch various promotional campaigns, drawing on a catalog of Promotion Types, such as discounts or special offers; this relationship is very versatile, allowing a restaurant to launch multiple promotions at the same time and the same type of promotion to be used by multiple restaurants in the network.
