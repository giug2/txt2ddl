This system was designed to map the creative and collaborative universe of music production, telling the story of how music comes to life. At the center of this world we find the MAIN ARTIST, the beating heart of every musical project. Each artist is defined by his sound identity, which is why he is closely associated with a unique and specific MUSICAL GENRE that characterizes his unmistakable style. However, great music is rarely a solitary endeavor. For this reason, each MAIN_ARTIST is the leader of a team, being able to count on the talent of numerous SUPPORT_MUSICIANS who make up his band or his team of collaborators. These musicians, in turn, also have their own specialization and identity, each of them being linked to a particular MUSICAL GENRE which defines their skills and artistic background. The magic of creation needs a physical place to manifest itself: the STUDIO_RECORDING. This is the creative laboratory where ideas become sound. In our system, each artist, whether main or supporting, is connected to a reference recording studio, which represents his favorite operational base. This means that while a single studio can become a vibrant crossroads of talent, hosting many different artists and musicians, each creative has their own "headquarters" where they feel most at home. In this way, a complete network of relationships emerges that shows not only who creates the music, but also with whom and where, offering an overview of the dynamics that animate the music scene.
