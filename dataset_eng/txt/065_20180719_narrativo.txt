Welcome to a digital universe designed to explore and catalog the world's artistic heritage. This system was created to tell the stories behind each masterpiece, connecting the work to its creator and the place that houses it. Our journey begins on a global map, where each major `NATION`, identified by a unique `ABBREVIATION` and its full `NAME`, serves as a cradle for culture and talent. Within these nations, countless `CITIES` flourish, each with its own identifying `ID` and `NAME`, representing the nerve centers of artistic and cultural life. Each city, in turn, can host one or more `MUSEUMS`, true treasure chests of beauty, each recognized by a unique `ID` and its specific `NAME`.

Virtually entering one of these museums, we find ourselves walking through its `ROOMS`. Each room is uniquely defined by the combination of its `NAME` and the museum it belongs to, and we also know its `SIZE`. A museum is naturally made up of many rooms, but each room belongs unequivocally to only one museum. But who populates these spaces with their genius? The `ARTISTS`. Each artist in our archive has a personal `ID`, a `NAME` and a `SURNAME`, and is linked to his or her country of origin, demonstrating how each country has given birth to countless talents.

The beating heart of this system is, obviously, the `WORK OF ART`. Every single work is a unique treasure, cataloged with an exclusive `COD`, its `TITLE` and the `YEAR` of creation. The magic lies in the indissoluble bond between the work and its creator: an artist can give life to many works over the course of his career, but each masterpiece is the fruit of the genius of a single artist. Finally, we close the circle by placing the art in its space. A work of art finds its home within a specific room of a museum. This relationship is very flexible: a room can exhibit different works of art at the same time, and the same work, perhaps on the occasion of traveling exhibitions or loans, could be associated with multiple rooms over time, creating a dynamic and fascinating network that connects the creative genius to the world that admires him.
