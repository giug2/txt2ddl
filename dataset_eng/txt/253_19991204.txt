The schema presents a more detailed and normalized structure for a music catalog. The Song entity is now defined only by SongCode, Title and MusicGenre, with the latter acting as a foreign key to a new specific relationship, MusicGenre, described by GenreCode and Name. The relationship between songs and artists is managed through an associative table called Collaborations, containing the Song and Artist attributes. This table creates an explicit link with referential integrity constraints towards Songs and Artists, allowing a song to have multiple artists and an artist to be associated with multiple songs. The management of publications is also more structured: the Publications relationship, with its references to Song, RecordLabel, Album and the Year attribute, not only connects to Songs and RecordLabels but also to a new Album entity, defined by AlbumCode and Name. The descriptions of the entities EtichettaDiscografica (Name, Venue) and Artista (Artist Code, Surname, Name) remain unchanged in their structure compared to the previous scheme.
