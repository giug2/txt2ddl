So, basically here we keep track of some stuff that goes around the world of cinema. We have a list of actors, and for each one we write down the full name and give them a unique code, just so as not to confuse them. The same thing goes for directors, they too have a name and their own personal identification number. Then there are the cinemas, of which we just need to know what they are, so we identify them with a code and that's it.

The heart of it all is the films. Each film is very closely linked to a director; in short, a director can make a lot of films, but a film always has only one director behind it. As for actors, the thing is more flexible: a film can have a huge cast and, on the other hand, an actor can act in as many films as he wants. Finally, there is the link between films and the theaters where they are shown. Here too, there is a lot of freedom: the same film can be seen in lots of different cinemas, and a cinema, of course, can have quite a few different titles playing.
