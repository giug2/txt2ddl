So, basically we have a few things related to each other. There's stuff like works of art, each with its own code, a title and the year it was made. Each work is created by a single author, who has his own name and a code to identify him, but obviously an author can have created a lot of different works. Then, each work belongs to a certain type, such as "painting" or "sculpture", and one of these categories can contain quite a few works. These works are displayed in rooms, and each room can host several works, but a work is only in one room at a time. The rooms, which have a name and a size, are all part of a larger museum; a museum is full of rooms, but one room belongs only to that museum. Each museum, in turn, is located in a specific city, and a city can have a lot of different museums. The cities, then, are grouped by nation, so a nation has many cities, but a city is in only one nation. Oh, and it seems that the authors are also linked to a nation, probably the one of birth, so a nation can have many authors but an author is linked to only one nation.
