So, in practice we have this thing that we call Ateneo, which would be the university, identified by its code and with a name. Each university, then, is made up of quite a few Departments, and each of these has its own code to recognize it. Many different departments can obviously be part of the same university. Then there are the Students, each with their own Matriculation Number which makes them unique, and the various Degree Courses, which also have a code and a name to understand what they are about. When a student enrols, they are linked to only one Department and enroll in only one specific Degree Course. As a result, a lot of students may end up in the same department or take the same course. There is also a separate entity for Enrollments, which are distinguished based on the Academic Year, just to keep track of when they took place.
