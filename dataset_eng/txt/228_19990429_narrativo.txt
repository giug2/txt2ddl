This system was designed to offer a complete and detailed view of the life cycle of each product, from its product classification to its origin and storage. At the heart of it all is a solid catalog structure, organized hierarchically to ensure order and clarity. Each item belongs to a general `Category`, identified by a specific `Code` and `Name`, such as "Clothing" or "Electronics". For a greater level of detail, each `Category` is divided into numerous `Subcategories`, each uniquely defined by the combination of its own `Code` with that of the parent category to which it belongs.

The `Product` itself is the beating heart of the system. Each product is unmistakably identified by its `SKU`, but its uniqueness is strictly linked to the `Subcategory` it belongs to, ensuring that there is no ambiguity in the catalogue. An interesting feature is the ability of a product to be linked to many other products, allowing you to create complex relationships such as kits, bundles or suggestions for related accessories. To manage the supply chain, the `Supplier` comes into play, registered with his `VAT number` and `Company name`. The system intelligently associates each product `Category` with the suppliers who are able to source it, creating a clear map of who supplies what.

Traceability is fundamental: each `Product` is not an abstract entity, but is concretized in physical batches. Each production `Lot` is meticulously tracked via a unique `Lot Code`, and for each lot we know the `Available Quantity` and the `Production Date`. This allows you to follow every single piece from its birth. Each batch is unequivocally linked to a single `Supplier`, thus ensuring that you always know who supplied that specific batch of goods. Finally, logistics is managed through the `Warehouse` entity, identified by a `Code` and localized by a precise `Address`. The system keeps track of which warehouse is used by each supplier, closing the loop from manufacturer to storage and offering a complete and integrated view of the entire supply chain.
