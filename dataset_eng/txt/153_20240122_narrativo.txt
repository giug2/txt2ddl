Welcome to the beating heart of our academic management system, a digital universe designed to orchestrate complex and fascinating university life. Each path of knowledge starts from a `FACULTY`, the parent company that defines a specific area of ​​study. Each faculty is responsible for curating and offering a wide range of specialist 'COURSES'; although a faculty can give rise to countless courses, each individual course belongs uniquely to a single faculty, thus ensuring a clear organizational and academic structure. The protagonist of this world is the `STUDENT`, who has the freedom to personalize his own study plan by enrolling in a variety of `COURSES`. At the same time, each course becomes a dynamic learning environment, attended by numerous students from different backgrounds. This flexible relationship allows each student to build a tailor-made training experience and each course to be a crossroads of ideas and people. The knowledge is transmitted by the `PROFESSOR`, a key figure who can dedicate his expertise to teaching more `COURSES` during the year. In a similar way, a course, especially if complex or interdisciplinary, can be enriched by the intervention of different professors, each bringing a unique perspective. This creates a dynamic bond that reflects the collaboration and richness of the teaching staff. Finally, the system models the fundamental interaction between mentor and disciple: a `PROFESSOR` guides and evaluates countless students through the various disciplines he teaches, while a `STUDENT`, over the course of his career, will have the opportunity to learn from many different professors, absorbing a plurality of approaches and knowledge. This dense network of connections represents the very fabric of the academic community, a continuous dialogue that encourages growth and discovery.
