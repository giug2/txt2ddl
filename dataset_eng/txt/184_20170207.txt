The system describes the management of orders placed by customers of an online store.
At the center of the model is the customer, uniquely identified by his customer code and characterized by a name and shipping address.
Each customer is associated with one or more orders; each order, identified by its own ID, reports a total amount and refers to the code of the customer who placed it.
Furthermore, the order is linked to a specific courier, identified by its code, through which the shipment takes place.
The structure of each order is detailed by a series of detail lines, each with its own unique ID, a specific price for that item and the date of entry.
Basically, each detail line is linked to a specific product via the product code, thus creating a direct link with the product entity, which associates this code with its description.
