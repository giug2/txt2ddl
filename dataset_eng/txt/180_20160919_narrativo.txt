Welcome to the beating heart of our musical universe, a system designed to capture and organize the ephemeral magic of music festivals. At the center of everything is the great event, the FESTIVAL, each edition a unique entity that acts as a stage for countless sound experiences. A festival, in fact, is not a single act, but a vibrant mosaic made up of numerous and distinct PERFORMANCES. Each performance that takes place within a festival is a moment in itself, a chapter in the history of that event.

To fully understand the nature of a performance, our system defines it through an indissoluble link with a precise MUSIC_GENRE. This means that each performance is dedicated to one and only genre, be it rock, jazz, electronic or folk, thus guaranteeing a clear identity to each set. During this specific performance, a set list of songs is performed; in fact, the system connects each single performance to a multiplicity of musical SONGS, capturing the list of tracks that animated that particular moment of the festival.

Finally, to complete the picture, the creators of all this music enter the scene: the ARTISTS. In our world, an ARTIST is not confined to a single label, but is seen as a multifaceted talent. For this reason, each artist can be associated with different MUSICAL GENRES, reflecting his versatility and his creative path. In this way, the system not only documents which songs are played in which genre of performance and at which festival, but also celebrates the stylistic richness of the artists who animate the music scene.
