Welcome to the beating heart of a cinematic universe, a system designed to map and celebrate the intricate connections that bring the seventh art to life. At the center of this digital world lies the fundamental entity: Film. Each film is a unique work, identified by a specific code, and represents the fulcrum around which all the other stories revolve. The birth of a film, however, is never an isolated event; it is the result of a person's vision. For this reason, every film is inextricably linked to its creator, the Director. A director, recognized by his full name and a unique identifier, may direct numerous works over the course of his career, but each individual film bears the unmistakable signature of a single conductor. But what would a film be without its actors? This is where the Actors come on stage. Each actor, also identified by a code and name, lends his face and talent to bring the characters to life. The relationship between films and actors is a dense and dynamic network: a film can boast an ensemble cast made up of many actors, and, in the same way, an actor can build his own filmography by participating in countless different productions, creating a flexible and multifaceted bond. Finally, the cinematic experience is completed in the magical place where stories come to life on the screen: the Cinema. Each room, identified by its own code, is a portal to new worlds. Here too, the relationship is rich and complex, since a successful film can be shown in a multitude of different cinemas, while each cinema, over the course of the year, offers its audience a varied programming that includes many different films. This system, therefore, is not a simple collection of data, but a living map that tells how talents, works and places intertwine to create the magic of cinema.
