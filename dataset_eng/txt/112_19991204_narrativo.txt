Welcome to our fleet management system, a digital archive designed to map the fascinating world of vehicles, from their creators to their owners. This system allows us to precisely navigate through the history and ownership of each individual car, telling the story that links an idea to a concrete object on the road. Our journey begins from the origins, with the Nations, each identified by a unique acronym and its full name. Each nation can be the cradle of numerous car manufacturers, but each manufacturer, in turn recognized by its own identification code and famous name, has its roots in a single nation of origin. Each of these major car manufacturers creates a range of unique models; a model is unequivocally defined not only by its name, but also by the company that produces it, forming an inseparable pair that guarantees its uniqueness, and is further classified based on its Category. In this way, a single house can boast a large collection of different models, but each model belongs exclusively to a single manufacturer. From the model project we move on to the concrete example: the Car. Each vehicle circulating on the roads is a unique entity, registered in our system with its specific license plate and year of production. This means that although there may be countless cars of the same model, each is a distinct and perfectly traceable instance. Finally, every car has an owner. In our system, each Person is registered with their personal data, such as name and surname, and uniquely identified by their Tax Code. The ownership link is clear and direct: a person can own multiple cars, enriching their collection, but each individual car is associated with one and only one owner, ensuring simple and transparent ownership management.
