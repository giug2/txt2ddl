Imagine exploring the digital heart of an academic institution, a system designed to orchestrate the entire educational path in a harmonious and coherent way. At the center of this universe are people: on the one hand the Students, each identified by a unique code and registered with their full name, ready to embark on their journey into knowledge. On the other side we find the teachers, the guides of this path, who are also recognized through a personal identifier and their name. The bridge that connects these two communities is the fundamental entity of the Course. Each Course represents a specific subject of study and is uniquely defined not only by its own code, but also by the inseparable bond with the Teacher who holds it. This relationship is so close that a professor can naturally teach multiple courses, but each single course is entrusted to the responsibility of a single and specific teacher. The true academic dynamic comes to life in interactions: students have the freedom to compose their own study plan by enrolling in numerous different courses, and, at the same time, each course welcomes a class made up of a multitude of students, creating a lively learning community. Finally, teaching requires a physical space, the classroom. The system also manages this dimension with great flexibility, allowing a course to take place in multiple classrooms, perhaps for theoretical lessons in a large hall and laboratories in smaller spaces, while, vice versa, each single classroom becomes a multifunctional place which hosts the lessons of various courses over the course of the week. In this way, the system weaves together the stories of students, teachers, subjects and places, creating a complete and dynamic picture of academic life.
