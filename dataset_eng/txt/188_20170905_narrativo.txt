This system was designed to tell the stories of the world of sport, creating a rich, interconnected digital archive that captures the essence of teams, players and their evolution over time. The beating heart of this universe is the TEAM, each club is uniquely identified and characterized by its name and the historic date of foundation, which marks the beginning of its journey. Each team, however, does not exist in a vacuum, but has deep roots in a specific geographical context. For this reason, each TEAM is inextricably linked to a specific CITY, which in turn belongs to only one NATION, thus creating a clear map that places each club in the world. A nation can host numerous cities and, similarly, a city can be the birthplace of multiple teams, each with its own identity.

The life of a team is marked by competitive seasons. To represent this dynamic, the system introduces the concept of SEASONAL ROSTER, a snapshot that photographs the composition of a team for a specific year or season. Each team therefore has a history of squads, one for each season in which it participated, allowing you to follow its transformation over the years. The true connective tissue of this world is the relationship between the roses and the PLAYERS. A player, defined by his first and last name, can be part of many different seasonal squads throughout his career, wearing the shirts of multiple teams or remaining loyal to the same club for multiple years. At the same time, each seasonal roster is made up of a collection of numerous players, who join forces to compete in that specific season. Finally, to fully understand the path of an athlete, the system keeps track of his origins: each PLAYER comes from a YOUTH SOCIETY, the nursery where his talent was discovered and cultivated. Each youth club can launch the careers of countless talents, but each player is associated with a single academy of origin, thus celebrating the starting point of his sporting adventure.
