Welcome to the beating heart of our organization, an information system designed to precisely orchestrate every aspect of our strategic initiatives. This is not a simple data archive, but a dynamic map that connects ideas, people, places and resources to transform projects into tangible successes.

At the heart of it all is the concept of **Project**, each unique initiative, identified by a `Project Code` and described by a `Project Name`, represents our innovation engine. We understand that work does not happen in a vacuum, but in very specific physical locations. For this reason, each project can extend across multiple **Operational Locations**, each with its own unique `Location Code` and a specific address defined by `Street` and `Civic Number`. Our geographical presence is mapped with a clear hierarchy: each office is located in a specific **City**, recognized by its `ISTAT Code` and `CittaName`, which in turn belongs to a **Region**, defined by a `RegionCode` and a `RegionName`. This way, we can visualize exactly where our efforts are focused, knowing that a region hosts multiple cities and a city can accommodate several of our offices.

An ambitious project is achieved by breaking it down into smaller and more manageable parts: the **Tasks**. Each `Task`, with its `TaskCode` and `TaskName`, represents a specific activity. Our system flexibly links tasks to projects, allowing you to define a specific `Estimated Budget` for each combination, recognizing that a project is made up of multiple activities. To complete a task, **Resources** are required, which can be tools, technologies or materials, each identified by a `Resource Code` and a `Resource Name`. The system manages the complex allocation of these resources, knowing that a task can require several of them and a single resource can be fundamental for multiple tasks. Strategic planning, however, does not stop here: each resource is analyzed according to the potential **Risks** associated with it. Each `Risk`, with its `Risk Code` and `Risk Name`, is mapped onto the resources it could impact, creating a network of awareness that allows us to anticipate and mitigate critical issues.

Finally, the most precious element: people. Each **Team Member**, identified by his `Serial Number` and his `Surname`, is the true architect of success. The system does not simply record who they are, but tracks their participation in the various projects, specifying the exact `Allocation` of time and skills for each one. But a team is more than the sum of its individuals; it is an organized structure. For this reason, we have carefully modeled the **Hierarchical Relationship**, a link that clearly defines who is the superior and who is the inferior member within the structure. To add further detail, each hierarchical relationship is qualified by a **Relationship Type**, described in full, which clarifies its nature, allowing us to easily navigate the organization chart and understand the dynamics of collaboration and responsibility that animate our organization.
