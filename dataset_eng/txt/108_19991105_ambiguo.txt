So, basically here we have a system that revolves around novels. Every novel is very closely connected to an author, so much so that you can't think of a book without knowing who wrote it; obviously an author may have churned out a lot of different novels. Then, inside every novel you find characters. This is flexible: a book can have a lot of characters and, if desired, the same character could appear in multiple books, perhaps in a series. Finally, each novel is linked to only one publisher, the one who published it. So, a publishing house can have quite a few novels in its catalogue, but every single book comes out with only one brand. Essentially, there are separate lists for authors, novels, characters, and publishers, and everything is linked that way.
