Welcome to our academic management system, designed to map and organize the entire educational path offered by our institution. At the center of everything is the "COURSE OF STUDY", which represents the educational path chosen by a student, such as a degree in Computer Engineering or Classics. Each study course is a container that is divided into a series of fundamental stages: the individual "COURSES". These are nothing more than the specific lessons and teaching activities that make up the study plan. Each "COURSE" is intrinsically linked to one and only one "SUBJECT", thus defining the specific topic covered, be it "Mathematical Analysis" or "Roman History". The "PROFESSORS" guide the students in learning these disciplines. In our system, a professor is an expert who can be responsible for teaching several different subjects, but each individual subject is entrusted to the expertise of a single reference professor, thus guaranteeing clear and authoritative guidance. Finally, to complete the teaching framework, each course offered has an associated list of reference "TEXTS". A single course may require the consultation of numerous books and materials, which constitute the essential bibliography to delve deeper into the subject and prepare adequately. In this way, the system creates a clear and interconnected information network, where each study path branches out into specific courses, each course deals with a specific subject taught by a dedicated teacher and supported by selected texts.
