The data model defines two main entities: Project and Employee. The Project entity is uniquely identified by the ProjectCode attribute, which constitutes its primary key. The Employee entity is identified by the primary key Serial Number and has the additional attributes Surname, Name and HireDate. There is a many-to-many (N:N) cardinality relationship between the two entities, indicating that a project can involve multiple employees and an employee can be assigned to multiple projects. This association is qualified by its own attribute, Role, which describes the specific function of the employee within the context of a single project.
