This system was designed to efficiently and transparently orchestrate the entire company workflow, starting from customer management up to the implementation of individual operational tasks. At the center of our universe are Customers, each identified by a unique code and description. To better understand our market and personalize our services, each Customer is classified within a single and specific sector, such as the technological or financial sector, although the same sector can naturally group together numerous customers.

When a Client entrusts us with a task, a Project is born. Each project is intrinsically linked to a single Client, ensuring that each initiative has a clear and defined client; on the other hand, a Client can entrust us with multiple projects over time. To monitor progress and ensure impeccable management, each Project is entrusted to the responsibility of a single company Department, which will be responsible for its execution, while being able to manage several other projects at the same time. Furthermore, at any moment, a project is in a very specific State, which describes its life cycle phase, such as "In analysis", "In progress" or "Completed", allowing everyone to have an immediate vision of its progress.

The true essence of a Project lies in its breakdown into concrete and measurable activities, which we call Tasks. A project, in fact, comes to life through the set of its Tasks, and each single Task belongs exclusively to a single project, thus forming an inseparable bond. To optimize resources and concentrate efforts where it is most needed, each Task is assigned a specific Priority level. This means that, although many tasks may share the same urgency, each individual Task will have its own clear classification of importance, guiding teams towards the effective completion of project objectives.
