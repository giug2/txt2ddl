Welcome to the heart of our car rental management system, a platform designed to offer a flexible and tailor-made travel experience. At the center of our universe are our physical locations, the Branches, each identified by a unique code and located in a specific city, ready to welcome customers. Each branch manages a large and varied fleet of vehicles, and every single vehicle belongs exclusively to one of them, thus guaranteeing impeccable organization of the car fleet in the area. Each Vehicle is a unique entity, recognizable by its license plate and an identification code, and is described in detail through information such as the model, the segment to which it belongs, the number of seats, the year of registration and the engine, to allow the customer to always choose the solution best suited to their needs.

Customization is fundamental, which is why each Vehicle can be enriched with different Types of Equipment: whether it is a leather interior, an advanced navigation system or a roof rack, a vehicle can have multiple options and, in the same way, a certain type of equipment can be available on numerous models in our fleet. To make the offer even more attractive, our vehicles can be associated with various types of promotions. The same car could be part of both a weekend offer and a long-term promotion, just as a single promotional campaign can be applied to an entire category of vehicles, offering maximum commercial flexibility.

Our pricing system is dynamic and is based on the Rental Rate, which defines a specific daily amount. The genius of the system lies in the way this fee is calculated. This is not a fixed price, but a value that depends on multiple factors. In fact, each vehicle can have different rental rates associated with it, each designed for a different context. The periods of the year, such as high season, low season or holidays, contribute to determining these rates, each of which can define a series of specific rates. Furthermore, the cost may vary based on the Additional Services chosen by the customer, such as child seats or supplementary insurance; each service, in fact, contributes to defining a set of dedicated tariffs. In this way, the system is able to generate a precise and contextual daily cost, combining the selected vehicle with the rental period and any extra services requested, guaranteeing transparency and an offer that is always perfectly aligned with the needs of the moment.
