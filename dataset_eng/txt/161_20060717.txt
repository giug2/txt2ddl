The system is structured around the concept of Warehouse, each uniquely identified by a Warehouse ID and characterized by a Location Name.
Each warehouse is associated with a specific Supplier and a reference Courier.
The Supplier entity is in turn defined by a SupplierID, a Company Name and an AverageSupplyCost,
while the Couriers are described by a CourierID and a CompanyName.
The logistics of shipments is managed through Routes, which connect a Geographical Area, identified by an AreaID, to a Courier, via CorriereID, specifying a Rate for each route.
Naturally, each Geographic Area is defined by its own AreaID and AreaName.
In each Warehouse it is possible to find one or more Products, each registered with a unique SKU, a Product Name, a Description, a Support Contact and its Availability.
Associated with each product there is the possibility of a Product Variant, defined by the SKU of the reference product, a Variant Code and a specific Sales Price.
Finally, the system includes a bidding mechanism.
There are different types of benefits cataloged in the Promotion entity through a PromotionID and a PromotionName.
The Effective Promotion is applied by linking a promotion type (PromotionID) to a specific product (SKU), thus establishing a valid discount for that specific item.
