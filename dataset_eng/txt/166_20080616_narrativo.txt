Imagine embarking on your academic journey: this system is the digital map that guides your every step, from day one to graduation. At the center of this universe is you, the Student, a unique individual identified by an unmistakable Student Number, with your personal data such as Name, Surname and a telephone number for important communications. Your training path takes shape through a Study Plan, a personalized container, identified by its specific Plan_ID, which outlines the path you will follow. Flexibility is fundamental: the same student could have multiple study plans over time, and a plan model can be shared by many, creating common paths.

This Study Plan is not a monolithic block, but a mosaic made up of many pieces, called Course in the Plan. Each of these rows, identified by a Row_ID, represents a teaching that you have chosen or that has been assigned to you. To give meaning and structure to your path, each course included in the plan is classified according to a precise Type, which through a Code and a Description allows you to understand whether it is a fundamental exam, an optional one, or a laboratory.

But a course is not just a name on a plan; it is a living experience, with lessons and timetables. For this reason, each Course in the Plan is connected to a specific Course Session, i.e. the concrete appointment on the agenda, defined by a specific Day_Week and Time. This session is the practical expression of an official Teaching, the heart of the academic subject, defined by its own Teaching Code. A single Course, such as "Mathematical Analysis", can have multiple sessions throughout the week to accommodate all students.

Each teaching, in turn, is rooted in the organization of the university. It belongs to a specific Department, identified by a Code and a Name, which guarantees its quality and academic coherence. Finally, learning takes place in a physical place: each teaching takes place in a specific classroom, with its own Code and Name, which is located within a university location, also identified by a Code, a Name and the City in which it is located. In this way, the system not only defines the "what" you study, but guides you precisely towards the "where" and "when" of your education, orchestrating every detail of your university world.
