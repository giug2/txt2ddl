Two distinct logical schemes for managing bibliographic information are presented 
The central Books entity described by the Code Title Genre and Author attributes 
In this configuration each book is associated with a single author via a referential integrity constraint that links the Author attribute to the Writers relationship 
The Writers entity in turn defined by Code, Surname and Name 
Publication information is managed by the Editions relationship which includes Book Publisher Series and Year attributes 
This table establishes a link to both the Books relationship via the Book attribute and the Publishers relationship via the Publisher attribute 
Finally, the Publishers entity is characterized simply by Name and City
