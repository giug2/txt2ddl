Welcome to the heart of our library management system, a digital universe designed to bring stories to life and connect readers with knowledge. At the center of this world lies the concept of **BOOK**, each work is unique, unequivocally identified by its ISBN code and characterized by its TITLE. But a book is not born by itself; comes to life from the creativity of one or more **AUTHORS**. Our system recognizes this collaboration, allowing a single book to be associated with multiple authors and, vice versa, for one author to be the creator of numerous works. Each author is registered with a personal code, together with his/her personal data such as name, surname and date of birth. Furthermore, to better contextualise their work, each author can be linked to a single affiliate **ORGENCY**, such as a university or research institute, which in turn is identified by a code and a name, and can group together many talents under itself.

Once written, a book's journey continues with publication. Each book in our catalog is linked to a single **PUBLISHER**, a specific publishing house, identified by its own code and name, which can obviously have a vast number of titles in its catalogue. However, a literary work is an idea, but it needs a physical form to be read. This is why we distinguish the book as an intellectual work from its material incarnation, the **COPY**. Each book title may exist in multiple physical copies, each individually tracked via a unique INVENTORY number and with its own date of purchase, testifying to its entry into our collection.

Finally, the cycle is completed with the reader, our **USER**. Each user of our service is registered with an identification code, name, surname and telephone number. It is precisely the interaction between the user and the physical copy that represents the beating heart of the library: lending. A user can borrow multiple copies at the same time, but a specific copy can only be entrusted to one user at a time. To manage this flow flawlessly, each loan between a copy and a user is enriched by fundamental information: the return date, which allows us to know when that paper treasure will be available again for a new reader, ready to start another journey.
