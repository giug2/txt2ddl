The scheme describes three main entities: student, degree course and department.
The student entity, identified by a unique serial number, is characterized by the StudentName, Address and CourseYear attributes.
The department entity is defined by a code and the name of the department itself.
Finally, the degree course entity, identified by the course code and which has a CourseName attribute, is linked to a department. It is important to note that in specific contexts, such as for academic analyzes X and Y, the structure of these entities undergoes variations.
In these cases, the student, indicated as relationship R0, is described solely by StudentName and Address, thus omitting the CourseYear attribute.
Similarly, the degree course, indicated as relation R1, is enriched with the "TipoLaurea" attribute, thus being defined by NomeCorso, TipoLaurea and the department code.
