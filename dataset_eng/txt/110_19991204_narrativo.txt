This system was designed to create a complete digital archive of the automotive world, connecting vehicles, their owners and the manufacturers who created them. At the center of this universe are individual people, each identified unequivocally by their own Tax Code, and whose name and surname we also record. Every person has the possibility of owning one or more cars, establishing a clear and direct ownership link.

Each car, in turn, is a unique entity, recognizable by its license plate, which acts as a digital fingerprint in our system; we also keep the year of production of it. However, a car is not just an object in itself, but the exemplar of a larger project: the model. In fact, every single car belongs to a single and specific model, such as a "Panda" or a "Golf".

The models are defined by a name and a category to which they belong, but their true uniqueness derives from the indissoluble bond with the car manufacturer that produced them. This means that a model is identified not only by its name, but also by the manufacturer to which it belongs. Finally, above all, we find the manufacturers, each with its own identification code, name and country of origin. Each of these large companies can create numerous different models, but each model is the fruit of the genius and engineering of a single car manufacturer, thus closing a circle that goes from the large manufacturer to the individual owner.
