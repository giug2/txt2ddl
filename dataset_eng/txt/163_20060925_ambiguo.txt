In practice, we have a system that manages a series of musical events, let's say festivals. Each festival has its own identification code, a name, and start and end dates. Then, obviously, there are the artists, who also have a unique code, and for whom we know what musical genre they make and more or less what year they formed. The stages cannot be missing, each with its own code, a name to recognize it and an idea of ​​how many people it can contain. The core of it all is the performance, which is kind of a weird connection: basically, a performance is defined by the combination of a certain festival and a certain artist playing there. So, a single festival can host a lot of different performances, and similarly an artist can do multiple performances. Even a stage, of course, is linked to many performances, given that multiple artists perform on it during the event.
