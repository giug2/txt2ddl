This system was designed to precisely trace the complete journey of a product, from its conception to its final owner, creating a detailed story for each individual piece. At the highest level, everything is organized into Product Categories, each identified by a unique code and a name, which group products into macro-areas such as "electronics" or "clothing". Within each category, different brands thrive, each with its own identifier and name, but inextricably linked to a single category to which it belongs. Each brand, in turn, gives life to a multitude of specific Products; a product is uniquely defined not only by its name, but also by the brand that produces it, and has its own descriptive typology. The true heart of the system lies in the transition from the concept of the product to the single physical object: the Article. For each product model, in fact, there can be countless items, each absolutely unique and unrepeatable, identified by a serial number and with its own production date. The journey of each item ends when it meets a Customer. Each customer, registered with his own identifier, name and surname, can own numerous items, but each single item, with its serial number, can be associated with only one customer, thus guaranteeing impeccable ownership traceability.
