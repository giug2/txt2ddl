This system was designed to map and manage a company's entire organizational structure, offering a clear view of how people, departments and work activities interconnect to achieve common goals. At the top of everything we find the Company entity, the beating heart of the organization. Each company in the system is unique, identified by its own `company_id` code and characterized by its `company_name`. A company, by its nature, is a complex entity, which is why it is divided into different operating divisions. Each division, such as marketing or research and development, is identified by a `division_id` and a `division_name`, and is unambiguously tied to a single parent company. While a single company can house numerous divisions, each division belongs exclusively to a single company, creating a clear hierarchical structure. The Employees, the people who animate the organization, work within each division. Each employee is registered with his/her personal data, `name` and `surname`, and has an `employee_id` which, combined with the identification of the division to which he or she belongs, makes him or her unique in the system. This structure emphasizes that each employee is formally assigned to one and only one division. At the same time, the work materializes through the Projects. Each project, defined by a `project_name` and a `project_id`, is created and managed within a specific division. A division may be responsible for multiple projects at the same time, but each individual project is under the jurisdiction of a single division. The true operational dynamic emerges in the connection between employees and projects: a single employee can dedicate his talent to multiple different projects and, at the same time, a complex project almost always requires the collaboration of a team made up of multiple employees. This flexible relationship is the engine of collaboration, allowing you to assemble dynamic teams and allocate human resources efficiently depending on the needs of various business initiatives.
