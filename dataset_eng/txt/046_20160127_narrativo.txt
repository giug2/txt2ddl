Imagine a system designed to guide customers through a clear and organized shopping experience, whether within a large shopping center or a network of stores distributed across the territory. At the center of this universe are our stores, each identified by a unique code to never create confusion. For each store we know the commercial name, the exact address to reach it and even the floor on which it is located, making the search simple and immediate. The beating heart of the commercial offer is represented by the brands, each with its own identification code and, naturally, its distinctive name, which evokes its style and quality. To bring order to this variety, we have introduced product categories, also defined by a specific code and name, such as "Clothing", "Electronics" or "Catering". The structure is designed to be incredibly intuitive: each category can group together numerous different brands, offering an overall view of the offer. However, to ensure maximum clarity, each individual brand belongs to one and only one category, thus clearly defining its main identity. The link between brands and stores reveals the specialized nature of this system: it was designed to manage a network of sales points in which each individual store is dedicated exclusively to one single brand. This means that although a successful brand may have multiple flagship stores in different locations, when a customer enters one of these stores, they are guaranteed to find the assortment and authentic experience of that specific brand. In this way, our system is not just a database, but a real map that guides the user from a generic need, expressed by a category, to the discovery of specialized brands, until leading him to the door of the physical store perfect for his needs.
