This system was designed to precisely orchestrate and manage every detail of complex events, such as conferences, symposia or technology trade shows. At the center of everything is the EVENT, the main container of each activity, uniquely identified by its EVENT_DATE and characterized by a start time and a responsible organizer. An event, however, is not a monolithic block, but a mosaic of moments of knowledge and sharing. Each of these moments takes the form of an INTERVENTION, which can be a presentation, a workshop or a round table, each with its own specific title and a keynote speaker. To give a logical and thematic structure to the day, the interventions are grouped into different SESSIONS, such as "Artificial Intelligence" or "Sustainable Development". The link between interventions and sessions is very flexible: a session can host numerous interventions and, vice versa, a particularly relevant intervention could be presented in multiple sessions. To ensure flawless planning, the system records the exact time each intervention takes place within a specific session. Behind every intervention there are people and organizations who make their contribution. The system manages a registry of the SPEAKERS, each with their own name and an identification code, and of the COMPANIES they represent, also identified by a name and a unique code. The real strength of the system lies in how it connects these elements. For each speech, we do not limit ourselves to indicating a single speaker, but each PARTICIPATION_SPEAKER is defined in detail. This fundamental entity creates a precise link between a speaker, the company he belongs to and the specific role he plays in that speech, such as "Keynote Speaker", "Panelist" or "Moderator". Thanks to this granular structure, a single speech can be associated with multiple speakers from different companies, each with a well-defined role, thus capturing the complexity and collaborative richness of modern events.
