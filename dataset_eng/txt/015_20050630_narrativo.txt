Welcome to the heart of our hotel management system, a platform designed to organize and present the tourist offer of an entire area in a clear and complete way. Our world is structured around picturesque locations, which we call Hamlets, each identified by a unique code and known by its characteristic name. Numerous hotels can arise in each hamlet, creating an indissoluble bond whereby each hotel belongs to a single hamlet, but a hamlet can host multiple accommodation facilities.

Each hotel is an entity in itself, with its own identification code, a name, a precise address and a classification based on the category. To give you an idea of ​​its capacity, we record the total number of beds available and, of course, essential contact details such as telephone and fax. Variety is the spice of hospitality, so each hotel can offer different types of rooms, such as "single", "double" or "suite", defined by a specific description and a unique code. Likewise, one room type may be available in multiple hotels. To manage the flexibility of the packages, each structure can offer different price variants, such as "full board" or "room only", which are also uniquely described and identified, and available in multiple hotels at the same time.

The beating heart of the system is the definition of the Rates. Each tariff, with its specific price and identification code, is the result of a combination of factors. First of all, each rate is set by an individual hotel. This price, however, is not fixed, but depends on the Season in which you book, whether it is "high", "medium" or "low", each defined by a name and a code. Finally, the rate is strictly linked to a specific Service offered, such as "breakfast included" or "spa access", which is also identified by a name and a unique code. In this way, the system is able to precisely calculate the cost of a stay, combining the chosen structure, the time of year and the desired services in a single, clear rate.
