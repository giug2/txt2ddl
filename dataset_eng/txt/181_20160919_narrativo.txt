This system was designed to capture and organize the vibrant universe of concerts and live performances. At the center of everything is the ARTIST, the beating heart of every performance, identified by a unique code and described by a series of personal data. An artist's career is marked by TOURs, real musical adventures that group together a series of shows under a single name and a specific identifier.

Each tour takes the form of a series of EVENTS, which represent individual concerts. An event is a unique moment, defined by a combination of three key elements: the tour it is part of, the LOCATION in which it takes place and a repeat number, essential to distinguish multiple shows held in the same location within the same tour. Furthermore, each event can have a specific name that characterizes it. The LOCATION, in turn, is an entity in itself, like a stadium or a theatre, with its own identification code and name. A single artist can naturally be the protagonist of countless events, just as a location can host multiple concerts over time, but every single event is unequivocally linked to a single tour and takes place in a single location.

Finally, to immortalize the musical magic of an evening, the LADDER comes into play. Each playlist is a list of song titles, identified by its own code. Interestingly, the system associates each set list directly with a specific location, allowing you to record the different song sequences that have been performed over time in a specific place, thus creating a sort of historical musical archive for each venue. In this way, it is possible to know which songs have resonated within the walls of a particular stadium or theater during the various events held there.
