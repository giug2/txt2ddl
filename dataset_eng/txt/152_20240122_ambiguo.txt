So basically here we have a few things related to each other in the music world. There is the main artist, the big shot, who has his own code to be recognized. This artist is linked to only one type of music, a very specific musical genre, although obviously a genre can be played by a lot of different artists. Then, this main artist has his crew, quite a few support musicians who play with him, and each of these musicians, who also have their own identifier, works only for that artist. These support musicians are also associated with a single musical genre, probably the same as their leader. Finally, there is the recording studio, the place where they go to record, which also has its own code. It seems that both the main artist and his backing musicians are connected to one recording studio, as if it were their go-to place, even though the same studio can be used by a lot of different people.
