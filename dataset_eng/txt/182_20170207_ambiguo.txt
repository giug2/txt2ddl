So basically, we have a few things put together in this system. There are people, the owners, each with their own code to recognize it and obviously a name. Then, of course, there are the animals, also with a unique code and a name, you know, like Fido or Micio. Each animal, it seems, is assigned to a specific area, like a department of the clinic, and clearly a single department deals with a lot of different animals. In addition to this, each animal is linked to a kind of program, a treatment plan, which also has its own number and description. It seems that multiple animals can follow the exact same plan, but an animal only follows one at a time. The slightly strange part is the connection between the masters and these plans: a master can have quite a few different care plans under him, while each plan is linked to only one person. Perhaps because he has more than one animal, or who knows what other reason, the matter is not very clear.
