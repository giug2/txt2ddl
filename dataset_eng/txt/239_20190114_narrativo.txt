This system was conceived as a dynamic digital archive to narrate stories and careers in the world of sport. At the center of our universe are the Teams, each with its own unique identity defined by a Code, a Name that resonates in the stadiums and a Year of Foundation that marks its historical origin. Each team, however, is not an isolated entity, but is deeply rooted in a place; in fact, each club belongs to one and only one City, in turn identified by a Code, a Name and the country to which it belongs. The same city can be the birthplace of numerous teams, but each team has a unique territorial bond that defines its home. The true protagonists of this narrative are the Players, each registered with a unique Serial Number that distinguishes him throughout his career, together with his Surname and Name. A player's career and his relationship with clubs are shaped through Contracts. A contract is not a simple bond, but a fundamental chapter in the history of an athlete: it establishes the union of a single player with a specific team for a well-defined period, delimited by a Start Date and an End Date. Furthermore, each contract specifies the role that the player holds within the team, as attacker or defender, where each role is in turn cataloged with a unique Code and Name. To ensure the uniqueness of each contract, a player cannot start two different contracts on the same day. In this way, the system does not simply say who plays for whom, but reconstructs the entire chronology of a player's career and the historical composition of each team, showing how a player belongs to a team through a sequence of contracts that define his path over time.
