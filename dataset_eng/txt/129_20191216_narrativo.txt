Welcome to the beating heart of the world of skiing, an information system designed to celebrate and catalog the exploits of its greatest protagonists. At the center of this universe is the **Skier**, each athlete is a unique entity, identified by a personal code and described by his name and surname. But a champion does not arise from nothing; its roots are fundamental. For this reason, every skier is linked to his **City** of origin, a unique bond that tells us where his adventure started from. Of course, a city is not an island: each **City** belongs to only one **Nation**, creating a geographic map that allows us to explore the distribution of talent around the world. This means that a Nation can boast numerous cities that have given birth to great athletes, and consequently, each Nation is proud of its champions, establishing a direct and strong bond between the skier and his homeland. The arena in which these athletes compete is the world of skiing **Specialties**, each defined by its own code and name, such as downhill, slalom or super-G. The true magic of our system lies in connecting talent with discipline: a skier can excel in multiple specialties, and each specialty sees the participation of countless athletes. But we don't just register who participates; we keep track of the glory. For each skier, in each single specialty in which he competes, the system meticulously records the number of **Victories** achieved, allowing us to build a detailed palmarès and answer the question that every enthusiast asks himself: who is the greatest champion in that discipline? In this way, we intertwine each athlete's personal story with their sporting career, their place of origin and their incredible achievements on slopes around the world.
