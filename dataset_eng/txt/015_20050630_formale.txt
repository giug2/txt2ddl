The data model describes the management of hotel facilities and related rates. The `Frazione` entity, identified by `pk_frazione` and a `name`, groups hotels geographically. The `Hotel` entity, identified by `pk_hotel`, represents the accommodation facility and includes attributes such as `name`, `address`, `category`, `Total Number of Beds`, `telephone` and `fax`. There is a one-to-many relationship between `Village` and `Hotel`, indicating that each hotel belongs to only one village. The hotel is also associated with different types of offers via many-to-many relationships with the entities `Tipo Camera` (identified by `pk_tipo_variante_lavoro` and a `description`) and `TipoVariantePrezzo` (identified by `pk_tipo_variante_price` and a `description`). The core of the pricing system is the `Tariff` entity, identified by `pk_tariffa` and containing the `price` attribute. Each rate is uniquely determined by three one-to-many relationships: it is associated with a single `Hotel`, a specific `Season` (identified by `pk_season` and `name`), and a specific `Service` (identified by `pk_service` and `name`). In this way, the price of a rate is contextualized by hotel, period and service offered.
