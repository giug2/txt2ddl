So, we basically have a system that handles some college stuff. There are Departments, which are identified by a unique code, and then have a name, the year they were born and even the color of their logo, for some reason. Each department, of course, is located in some City, and these cities in turn belong to a Nation. For both cities and countries, we just keep track of a code and name, nothing complicated. The heart of everything are the Students, clearly. For each student we know his student number, which is the thing that identifies him, then the usual things such as name, surname, date of birth and also the average grade. There are also Degree Courses, each with its own code and specific name. The interesting thing is how it all ties together: a department is necessarily in one city, but a city can house a lot of different departments. The same goes for cities and nations. Then, a department has quite a few students enrolled, but each student reports to only one department. Furthermore, each student is associated with his or her city of origin, and obviously multiple students can come from the same place. Finally, many students may be enrolled in the same degree course, but each individual student, apparently, only follows one.
