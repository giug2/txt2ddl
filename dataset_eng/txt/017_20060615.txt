Show the conceptual scheme of a football tournament database, according to the following
specifications.
• The various tournaments have a code and a name.
• Each tournament is made up of a certain number of teams and a ranking that assigns to each team
a score.
• The teams participate in only one tournament and have a name and a roster of players whose names we record
shirt number, name and date of birth.
• Matches take place between two teams from the same tournament, on a certain date, in a certain stadium and have
a final result.
• We want to record the players who play in the various games and the role played (which is the same in one
match but may vary in different matches).
