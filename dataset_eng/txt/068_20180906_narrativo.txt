This system was designed to completely and fluidly manage the relationship with customers of a service, such as a telephone or energy supply, following every phase of their experience, from personal details to payment management. At the center of everything we find the `Person`, our customer, unequivocally identified by his Tax Code. We store essential information for each person such as surname, first name and date of birth.

Each customer can activate one or more services, which in our world we call `Users`. This means that the same person can be the holder of multiple contracts, for example for the home and for the office. Each `User` is unique, defined by its own identifying `Number` and associated with a specific physical `Address` where the service is provided.

The utilities are also organized on a territorial basis for more efficient management. In fact, each `User` belongs to an operational `District`, recognizable by a `Prefix` and a descriptive `Name`. The districts, in turn, are grouped into larger geographical areas, the `Provinces`, each defined by an `Acronym` and a complete `Name`, thus creating a clear territorial hierarchy.

The operational heart of the system is the billing cycle. For each individual `User`, the system periodically generates `Bills`. Each bill is a document in itself, with a `Code` that uniquely identifies it, a `Date` of issue and, naturally, an `Amount` to be paid. To complete the cycle, the system also precisely tracks incoming financial flows. A `Bill` can be paid through one or more `Payments`, a flexible solution that also allows you to manage installment payments. Each `Payment` is recorded with its own unique `Code`, the `Date` on which it was made and the `Amount` paid, thus guaranteeing perfect accounting reconciliation and an always updated view of the status of each customer's accounts.
