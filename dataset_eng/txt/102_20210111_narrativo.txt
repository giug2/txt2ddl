Imagine a management system designed to precisely orchestrate every aspect of a dynamic supermarket chain, a digital ecosystem where every element, from the individual product to the employee, finds its logical place. At the heart of this universe are the Supermarkets, each point of sale is a unique entity, identified by a specific code and clearly recognizable thanks to its name and address. To organize the infinite variety of items on the shelves, the system relies on well-defined product categories, such as "Dairy Products" or "Bakery", each distinguished by a unique acronym and a descriptive name. Each individual Product, in turn identified by its own code and name, belongs exclusively to one of these categories, creating an indissoluble bond that defines its nature. True flexibility emerges in the way a supermarket can offer a wide range of categories to its customers and, at the same time, a single category can be present in numerous stores in the chain, ensuring a diverse and widespread offer.

But the real driving force of every supermarket is the people: the Employees. Each team member is registered with a personal number, a name and a surname. The system is designed to manage a dynamic and interconnected workforce, allowing one employee to serve in multiple supermarkets and, of course, for each supermarket to benefit from the collaboration of many employees. To better understand our staff, we also track their origins and residence through a detailed geographic structure. The territory is organized into Provinces, each with its own code and name, which in turn group together various cities, also identified by a code and a name. Each employee is linked to the geographical fabric in two distinct and important ways: the system records both the city of birth and the city of current residence separately. This means that for each employee we know not only where they live, but also where they come from, allowing for more complete human resources management that is attentive to the territorial context in which the company operates.
