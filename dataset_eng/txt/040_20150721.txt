• for each subject there can be multiple courses, held by the same professor or by different professors
• each course is related to one and only one subject
• Each professor teaches zero or more courses
• each course has one and only one professor and is offered for one and only one course of study
• for each course of study there are one or more courses of a given subject, distinguished by a progressive number indicating
the “channel” (e.g.: Databases, channel 1)
• all courses of a given subject have the same textbook (one and only one)
