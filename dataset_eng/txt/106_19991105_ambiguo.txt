So, we basically have a system for keeping track of some college stuff. There are the professors, each with their own identification code to avoid confusion, and then there are the study courses, in short the degrees, also with their own ID. We also have subjects, such as Analysis, Physics, etc., each with its own number to recognize it. The central piece is the actual courses, which are a bit of a mess: to identify one you have to put together its code, the subject it refers to and also the study course it is part of, otherwise it's not clear which one we're talking about. In fact, a lot of different courses are part of a single larger course of study, and a single subject can be broken up into many courses. The link between professors and courses is a nice mix: a professor can teach a lot of different courses, and at the same time a course can have more than one teacher, perhaps if they divide the program. Finally, there are the textbooks, each with its own code, and the thing is that sometimes a bunch of different courses can end up using the exact same textbook.
