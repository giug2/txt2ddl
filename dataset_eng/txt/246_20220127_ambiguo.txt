So basically here we have a system that keeps track of a few people at the university. There are professors, and to know who is who their personal code is not enough, but you also have to know which department and which campus they are connected to, it's a bit of a mess, their "license plate" is made up of all three of these pieces put together. Then, obviously, there are the departments, each with its own ID, and the same goes for the campuses, which have their own number to recognize them. This gets interesting because a professor can be connected to more than one department and, on the other hand, a department has a lot of professors in it, so there's a lot of back and forth between those two things. Finally, there are doctoral students, who are a bit like professors in how they are identified: their code is needed, but also that of the professor who supervises them and the campus to which they belong. The link here is closer, because each doctoral student reports to only one professor, but a professor can have a nice group of doctoral students under his wing.
