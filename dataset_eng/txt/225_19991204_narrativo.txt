This system was designed to tell the complete story of each individual product, from its conception by the manufacturer to its arrival in the hands of a customer. At the center of this universe are the Brands, each identified by a unique code, with a recognizable name and a country of origin that defines its identity. Each brand can give life to a wide range of Products, each of which is uniquely defined by the combination of its name and the brand to which it belongs, as well as being classified by a specific type. When a product model is actually manufactured, it turns into a concrete and tangible Article, a unique instance with its own serial number that distinguishes it from all the others and a year of production that marks its birth. Of course, a single product model can be made into countless identical items. The final phase of this journey sees the item become part of a customer's life. Each customer, registered in the system with a personal code, name and surname, can own numerous different items. However, each individual item, with its serial number, is tied to one and only one customer, thus creating a clear and traceable link that allows us to know exactly who owns which specific piece, completing the journey from manufacturer to end user.
