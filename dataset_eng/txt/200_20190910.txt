• each order relates to a customer and is made up of a certain number of order lines
• each customer has a customer code, surname and name
• each order line (which constitutes a “line” of the relevant order) has
– a product description, which is a simple text string
– a supplier (where the product comes from) which can be a manufacturer or a distributor, but we still assume it is a simple text string
– a delivery date and a price
