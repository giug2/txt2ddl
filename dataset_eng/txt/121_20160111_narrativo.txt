Welcome to the heart of our automotive management system, a world designed to catalog and track every vehicle from its initial concept through its physical existence. Our journey begins at the broadest level, the market segment, where each category, identified by a unique code and clear description, helps us position our vehicles, whether they are agile city cars or powerful SUVs. Each segment includes one or more car models, each defined by a specific code, its dimensions in terms of length and the number of seats it offers passengers. A model, however, is only the starting point; true customization takes shape in the version. Each model can be declined in numerous different versions, but each version belongs unequivocally to a single model. A version is characterized by its own code, a distinctive commercial name, the displacement of its engine and, of course, its list price. A fundamental aspect that defines each version is its type of power supply. Whether petrol, diesel or electric, each type is cataloged with a code and a description, and each version is associated with a single type of power supply, thus specifying its technological soul. Finally, from the project we move on to reality: every single car produced is a concrete instance of a specific version. Each car is unique and unrepeatable, unequivocally identified by its chassis number. We also know the color chosen and the exact date on which it was produced, thus completing the cycle that transforms a market idea into a tangible vehicle ready for the road.
