Imagine the energy of a large music festival, with thousands of people united by the same passion. Our system was designed precisely to orchestrate this complex and vibrant reality, managing every single detail to create an unforgettable experience. At the center of this universe is the **Festival**, the beating heart of every event. Each festival is a unique entity, identified by its specific `Code`, and is distinguished by its `Name`, taking place in a precise time frame, defined by a `Start Date` and an `End Date`.

But what would a festival be without its protagonists? Here comes the **Artists**. Each artist or musical group is registered in the system with a unique `Code`, allowing us to know their `Musical Genre` and `Training Year`, thus telling their musical story. Naturally, every large event needs its stage spaces. The system manages each **Stage** as a distinct location, also identified by a unique `Code`. For each stage we know its `Name` and, a fundamental fact for the organization, its `Maximum capacity' of spectators.

The true operational heart of the system, the moment in which all these elements converge, is the **Exhibition**. This entity does not represent a physical object, but the act of performance itself, creating an indissoluble and specific bond between a particular `Artist` and a particular `Festival`. In fact, each performance is uniquely defined by this very couple. Thanks to this structure, we know that a single festival can host countless performances and, similarly, an artist can delight the audience on multiple occasions. Finally, each individual performance is assigned to a specific 'Stage', letting you know exactly where the magic will happen. This means that one stage can be the scene of many different performances over the course of a festival, hosting different talents. In this way, the system precisely orchestrates the complex symphony of a musical event, ensuring that every artist finds its stage and that every festival can offer a rich and varied program of performances.
