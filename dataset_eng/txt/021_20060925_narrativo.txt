Welcome to the beating heart of our museum management system, a tool designed not only to archive data, but to orchestrate real artistic experiences. At the center of everything is the **Exhibition**, the main event, identified by a unique code and characterized by an evocative title, a start date and an end date that define its time frame. An exhibition, however, lives through the works that compose it. This is why each **Painting**, with its identification code, the signature of its **Author** and the **Year** of creation, is meticulously catalogued.

The act of giving life to an exhibition takes the form of **Exhibition**, a fundamental concept that inextricably links a specific painting to a particular exhibition. This entity represents the moment when a work of art is actually staged. To complete the setup, each exhibition is associated with a specific **Room** of the museum, in turn defined by a code, a name and a size, thus creating a physical and logical map of the event. Thanks to this structure, a single exhibition is made up of multiple works on display, each placed in its own room, and a single painting can be the protagonist of several exhibitions over time.

To guide visitors on a coherent and fascinating journey, we create thematic **Itineraries**. Each itinerary, identified by its own code, is nothing more than a reasoned path that connects a series of works exhibited in a precise sequence, defined by an order number. This allows us to offer paths such as "The masterpieces of the Renaissance" or "The evolution of abstractionism", where the same work can intelligently be part of multiple thematic paths.

The visitor experience takes shape through the **TipoVisita**. Each itinerary, in fact, can be offered to the public through different types of visits: perhaps a standard guided tour with a specific name, a pre-established time and duration, or a special tour with the curator. Finally, to manage reservations and attendance, we record every single **Visit**. Each type of visit can take place on multiple dates, and for each date we keep track of the number of participants, thus ensuring that each group can fully enjoy the artistic journey designed for them. In this way, the system transforms a simple collection of works into a living narrative, a curated and unforgettable experience for every visitor.
