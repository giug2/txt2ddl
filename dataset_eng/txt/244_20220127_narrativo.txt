Welcome to the heart of our musical universe, a system designed to map the infinite connections that bind the sounds, voices, and ideas we love. At the center of this world are the ARTISTS, the creative minds from which everything originates. Each artist is a unique entity, distinguished by his or her own personal identifier, and represents the starting point for every sonic adventure.

The creativity of an artist manifests itself mainly through ALBUMS, which are much more than simple collections of songs: they are real sound worlds, each with its own identity and atmosphere. In our system, an album is not defined only by its title or its cover, but is intrinsically linked to a single musical GENRE. This choice is fundamental: an album is born with a precise soul, whether rock, jazz, or classical, and its belonging to a genre defines its very identity. While a genre can span countless albums, each individual album is a pure expression of that specific musical style.

Naturally, an album comes to life through the SONGS that compose it. Each song is a fundamental piece of the mosaic, a chapter in the story that the album tells. A song cannot exist in isolation; its very essence is defined by the album it is part of. Therefore, each track is inseparably linked to its album of origin, and while an album may contain many songs, each song belongs exclusively to a single album.

The most dynamic and fascinating relationship is the one that binds artists to their albums. Music is a fluid and collaborative art, and our system reflects this reality. A single artist can have a huge discography, contributing to numerous albums over the course of their career. At the same time, an album can be the fruit of the genius of multiple artists who join forces, as in a duet, a band or a collective project. This flexibility allows us to capture the richness of musical collaborations, tracking solo careers and group projects with equal precision, creating a complex and beautiful web of talent and creativity.
