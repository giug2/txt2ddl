This system was designed to create a dynamic and well-organized digital market, where the interaction between sellers and buyers is clear and structured. At the center of everything we find the figure of the **Seller**, a unique and identifiable actor thanks to his personal code. Each seller has the opportunity to populate the market by creating one or more **Sales Advertisements**, which represent their specific offers to the public. Each advertisement is a complex and precise entity, uniquely defined not only by the **Product** it offers for sale, but also by the **Category** to which that product belongs and by a **Lot** identifier, which allows you to distinguish between different batches or versions of the same item. This means that a product, such as a "t-shirt", belonging to the "apparel" category, could be sold in different batches, perhaps with different prices or conditions, and each combination of these three elements generates a unique advertisement. Naturally, the system organizes all the items in a general catalogue: each **Product** has its own identification code and always belongs to a specific **Product Category**, ensuring logical and orderly navigation. To build a relationship of trust and transparency, the system also includes a feedback mechanism: each product can receive an unlimited number of **Reviews**, but every single review is unequivocally linked to the product for which it was written, thus offering potential buyers a clear and detailed vision of the experience of others.
