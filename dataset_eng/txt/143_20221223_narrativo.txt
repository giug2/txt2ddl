This system was designed to tell the stories of the sporting world, intertwining the stories of the teams, the careers of the athletes and their geographical origins. The beating heart of this universe is the `Society`, each club is identified by a unique `Code` and is distinguished by its official `Name`, the social `Color` that ignites the fans and its `Foundation` date, which marks the beginning of its history. Each `Society` has deep roots in a specific place, a `City` that hosts it and whose name it proudly bears; although a metropolis can be the cradle of multiple teams, each club is inextricably linked to a single `City` to which it belongs.

The cities, in turn, are not isolated but are part of a larger 'Nation', creating a clear geographical map. Many cities, each with its own `Code` and `Name`, may be within the same `Country`, but each `City` belongs to one and only one country, also identified by a `Code` and a `Name`.

Of course, no `Society` would exist without its protagonists: the players. Each `Player` is a unique entity, registered with a personal `Code`, a `Last Name` and a `First Name`. His physical characteristics, such as `Height` and `Weight`, complete his profile. The link between athlete and team is exclusive: a `Player` plays for only one `Club` at a given time, while a `Club` is made up of a squad of numerous players. On the pitch, each athlete plays a specific `Role`, which defines their position and tactical function. Several players can share the same `Role` (such as "attacker" or "defender"), but each individual `Player` specializes in only one position. Finally, to complete the picture, the system tracks the origins of each champion, linking each `Player` to their `Hometown`. This allows us to discover how the same location may have given birth to multiple talents, despite knowing that each athlete has a single, specific place of birth.
