Show the conceptual scheme of a database for the management of articles in a scientific journal, according to the following specifications.
• Articles have a title, subtitle, one or more authors and a text (a very large, but still manageable string)
• The authors have name, surname, email and affiliation (the institution for which they work)
• For each institution (of the authors), the name, address and country are of interest
• The magazine is published a certain number of times in a year. A year's publications come
collected in a volume (which is given an overall title). Each publication has a number, unique in
respective volume, a publication date and a series of articles, for each of which is recorded
the start page and the end page
