This system was designed to offer a complete and integrated view of our logistics network, precisely tracing the distribution of products across the national territory. At the center of our geographical structure are the **Provinces**, each uniquely identified by its acronym, such as `PROVINCE_ABSCRIPTION`, and described by its full name. Each province acts as a container for a set of **Municipalities**, each of which is registered with its own name and an official `ISTAT_CODE` which guarantees its uniqueness. It is in this territorial context that our **Warehouses** operate: each warehouse is a physical location with its own unique identifier, `WAREHOUSE_ID`, a specific name, `LOCATION_NAME`, and even the year in which it began its activities, `OPENING_YEAR`. The location of each warehouse is precisely defined, since each of them is associated with a single municipality, allowing us to always know where each of our facilities is located.

The heart of our business lives inside these warehouses: the **Products**. Each item we manage is cataloged with a `SKU` code which makes it absolutely unique, accompanied by its name, the brand it belongs to and the date of launch on the market. To maintain order and facilitate searching, each product belongs to a single **Category**, which, identified by a `CATEGORY_ID`, groups together items with similar characteristics under a single name. The link between logistics and the catalog takes the form of inventory management: a single warehouse can host a wide variety of different products, but each product, with its specific SKU, is exclusively assigned to a single reference warehouse, thus creating a clear mapping of where each item is primarily managed in our system.
