Welcome to the beating heart of our academic organization, a system designed to map and manage the entire universe of university knowledge and teaching. At the highest level, the university is structured into Departments, each identified by a unique code and a name, which represent the major areas of knowledge. Each Department acts as a home for a multitude of subjects, the individual study disciplines, also defined by a specific code and name, which constitute the fundamental building blocks of our educational offering.

These subjects come to life within the Degree Courses, the structured paths that students undertake to achieve their academic goals. Each course, with its code, name and detailed description, is a unique mosaic, made up of multiple subjects. The flexibility of the system allows the same subject, such as "Mathematical Analysis", to be a fundamental exam for multiple degree courses, from Engineering to Physics, creating a connective tissue between different disciplines.

The responsibility for teaching is entrusted to the Chairs, prestigious academic entities identified by a name, which have an office and are led by a Full Professor. A single Chair, representing an area of ​​excellence, can supervise and influence several degree programs. The assignment of teaching tasks is managed with precision through the Teaching Assignment, a formal link that unites a specific Teacher, recognized by his student number, name and surname, to a specific Chair. A teacher can hold multiple positions and a chair can make use of multiple teachers, ensuring complete teaching coverage. Furthermore, each teaching role is qualified by a well-defined Academic Role, such as "Associate Professor" or "Researcher", specifying the nature and level of responsibility of that particular task.

Finally, the system manages the crucial logistics of student life. Numerous Exam Sessions are planned for each degree course, each made unique by the combination of the date and the degree course to which it belongs, and characterized by a starting time. To ensure that each exam runs smoothly, each session is assigned to a specific room, identified by a code and name, ready to welcome students. Naturally, the same room can host several exam sessions over the course of the year, becoming a focal point of everyone's academic journey.
