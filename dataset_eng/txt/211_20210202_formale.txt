The data model describes a structure for academic management, divided into four main entities. The Department entity, uniquely identified by a Department Code (PK), has a Name attribute. There is a one-to-many relationship between Department and Degree Course, so a department can provide multiple courses. The CorsoDiLaurea entity is identified by the CourseCode (PK) and includes the Name and SedeDidattica attributes. In turn, the Student entity, identified by the Student Number (PK) and with a Name attribute, is linked to the Degree Course by a one-to-many relationship, indicating that a course can have multiple students enrolled. Finally, the Examination entity records student results and is identified by a primary key composed of IDVerbalizzazione and fk_MatricolaStudente. This entity also contains the Date and Vote attributes. The relationship between Student and Exam is one-to-many, allowing each student to take an indefinite number of exams.
