Welcome to the heart of a digital archive designed to navigate the vast and fascinating world of literature. At the center of this universe are the **Writers**, the creative minds from which everything originates. Each author is uniquely registered through a personal **Code**, which distinguishes him from anyone else, and we also keep his **Name** and **Surname** for easy identification. Their creations, the **Books**, are the true protagonists of our catalogue. Each book has its own identifying **Code**, a **Title** that evokes its content and a classification by literary **genre**. The link between the writer and what is written is fundamental: our system recognizes that a **Writer** can be the author of many different **Books**, and at the same time, a single **Book** can be the result of the collaboration of multiple authors.

However, a work does not reach the public without the work of a **Publisher**. Each publishing house in our system is uniquely identified by its **Name** and we know the **City** of origin. The relationship that binds a book to its publisher is crucial and defines its path in the world. Each **Book** is published by one and only **Publisher**, while, of course, a single **Publisher** can boast a catalog that includes countless books. This link is not a simple connection, but is enriched by specific details that tell the history of the publication: for each published book, we record the **year** in which it was published and the **publishing series** in which it was inserted, thus providing a precious context for each work.
