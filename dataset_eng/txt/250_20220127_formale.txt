The data model defines four main entities: PROFESSOR, COURSE, TUTOR and DEPARTMENT. The DEPARTMENT entity is identified by a primary key `department_id`. The COURSE entity is characterized by a composite primary key, made up of the `course_id` and `fk_department` attributes. The PROFESSOR and TUTOR entities are identified by the primary keys `professor_id` and `tutor_id` respectively. The system provides an N to 1 cardinality relationship between the COURSE entity and the DEPARTMENT entity, implemented through the foreign key `fk_department` in COURSE, so each course belongs to a single department, while a department can offer multiple courses. A ternary relationship with cardinality N:N:N is also specified that interconnects the PROFESSOR, TUTOR and COURSE entities, establishing a many-to-many-to-many association between them.
