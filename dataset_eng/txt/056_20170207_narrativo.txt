This system was designed to orchestrate the entire working life of a structured company, managing people, customers and activities in a harmonious and interconnected flow. At the heart of the organization we find the Departments, each identified by a unique code and a specific name, which represent the different functional areas of the company. Employees work within these units; each employee, with his name and identifier, is assigned to a single and specific Department, thus creating a clear belonging and hierarchical structure. At the same time, the company interacts with its clients, i.e. the customers who request the services. Each client, identified by a code and name, is in turn managed by a specific department, which means that a department can be the point of reference for multiple clients. Collaboration with customers takes shape in the creation of projects. A single Client has the right to start multiple projects, each with its own name and identification code, but each project is unequivocally linked to the client who commissioned it. Finally, to complete these projects, the system allows you to assign staff in an extremely flexible way: a project can require the collaboration of numerous employees and, at the same time, a single employee can contribute his or her skills to several projects at the same time, ensuring a dynamic and efficient allocation of human resources.
