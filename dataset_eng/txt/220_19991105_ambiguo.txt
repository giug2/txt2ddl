So basically here we have a few things that are talking to each other. There is a sort of list of Sellers, each with its own identification code to avoid confusion. Then there's the stuff they sell, the Products, and these also have their own unique number. Each product, among other things, comes from some Supplier; usually a lot of different products can be linked to the same supplier, who also has his own code. The heart of everything seems to be the Offer, a slightly strange thing that brings together a lot of pieces: to define it well you need its code, but also that of the product being offered, that of the seller who makes it and even the code of a Catalogue. In practice, a seller can make a bunch of different offers and a single product can appear in multiple offers. These offers then end up in a catalogue, and obviously a catalog can contain quite a few different offers. It's all a series of connections, where the offer is the meeting point between who sells, what is sold and where it is written.
