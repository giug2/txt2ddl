This system was designed to map and manage the organizational structure of a company in a clear and detailed way, relating people, operational units and physical locations. At the center of our model we find the figure of the manager, a key role for the organization. Each manager belongs to a single and specific department, creating a clear functional position; however, a single department can naturally group together multiple managerial figures. To support their activities, each manager can be associated with one or more dedicated assistants, establishing a direct relationship in which each assistant responds to only one manager. Consequently, assistants are also logically placed within a single department, that of the manager to whom they report. The entire structure is also anchored to the physical reality of the company through the offices: both managers and assistants are assigned to a specific work location, and the same office can host numerous managers and assistants, facilitating logistical management and understanding the distribution of personnel across the territory.
