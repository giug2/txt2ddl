This system was designed to orchestrate each patient's journey within a modern veterinary clinic with the utmost care and attention, placing the bond between animals and their human companions at the centre. At the heart of this ecosystem we find the **Owner**, registered with his unique identifier and name, who serves as the main point of reference for every interaction. Each owner is connected to his beloved animals. Each **Animal** is in turn a unique entity in the system, unambiguously identified by a code and known by its name, ensuring that each treatment is personalized and precise.

To guarantee specialized and targeted assistance, each animal is assigned to a specific **Clinical Area** of the facility, which can be surgery, internal medicine or any other specialist department. Each area is defined by a name and an identification code. While a single clinical area can handle numerous patients, each animal is associated with one and only one area of ​​expertise, thus ensuring a clear and defined therapeutic focus.

The healing path takes shape in the **Care Plan**, a detailed program identified by its own code and name, which outlines the necessary therapies and interventions. The system requires each animal to follow a single active treatment plan, but the same standard protocol, such as a cycle of vaccinations, can be applied to multiple different animals. The most significant relationship, however, directly links the owner to the therapeutic path: a single **Owner** can in fact be associated with multiple treatment plans. This structure reflects the reality that a person may own multiple animals, each with their own treatment path, or a single animal may have followed multiple plans over time, allowing the owner to maintain a complete, centralized view of all care provided.
