This system was designed to clearly and completely manage the tax payment process, putting the citizen at center stage. Each person, registered as a Taxpayer, is uniquely identified by their Tax Code and characterized by personal information such as Name and City of residence. Over time, each Taxpayer finds himself having to make payments which are formalized by completing one or more Payment Forms. Each Form is a separate document, with its own identifying ID code, and contains a Total amount to be paid. Of course, a single Taxpayer may complete and pay numerous forms over the years, but each specific form is unambiguously associated with only one person. The payment of a form takes place through a financial intermediary, a Bank, also registered in the system with its own Code and Bank Name. A bank can process an infinite number of forms from different taxpayers, but every single form is managed and receipted by a single bank. To ensure maximum transparency and detail, the Total amount of a form is not a single value, but is the sum of different expense items, represented as individual Lines. Each module, in fact, is made up of one or more lines, each with its own ID. Within each Row we find the specific Amount and the Reference Year for that payment, thus allowing us to distinguish the payments by annual accrual. Finally, to know exactly what you are paying, each Row is linked to a specific Tax, defined by a unique Code and its Tax Name. In this way, the system precisely tracks that a given line on a form refers to the payment of a certain tax for a given year, creating a complete and easily searchable picture of each citizen's entire payment history.
