Imagine a vibrant, comprehensive system designed to capture the essence of a major international film festival and its awards. Its aim is to weave together the stories of the talents, their works and the awards that celebrate their art, creating a rich and interconnected archive. At the center of this narrative universe are the Directors, each identified by a unique code and registered with their own name and surname. Their provenance is an important detail: each director is associated with a specific hometown, which, in turn, belongs to a nation, thus allowing us to draw a geographical map of the world's cinematic talent.

The works come to life through Projections. Each screening is a unique event, with its own identification code and a precise date on the calendar. To help the audience orient themselves, each screening is clearly classified according to its Film Genre, be it a thriller, a comedy or a documentary. Furthermore, we know exactly where the magic takes place: each screening is linked to a single Cinema, its screening room. The Cinemas themselves are the stages of this festival, each with its own name and identification code, and their location is fundamental; for this reason, each cinema is connected to the country in which it is located, offering a clear vision of the geographical distribution of the event venues.

The beating heart of the system is the Nomination, the moment in which art meets official recognition. This entity creates an indissoluble bond between a Director and a specific screening of his film, formalizing the nomination for an award. A director can of course accumulate several nominations over time, but each nomination refers to a single, specific screening. To give an international context, each nomination is also associated with the nation hosting and awarding the award. Finally, the most awaited information, i.e. whether the Award Obtained was actually won, is recorded for each individual nomination, transforming a simple nomination into a potential victory and thus completing the story of a film and its creator's journey towards glory.
