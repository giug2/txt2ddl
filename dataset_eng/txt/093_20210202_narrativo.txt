Welcome to an information system designed to capture the very essence of the world of catering, a universe where geography, people and flavors intertwine in a complex but fascinating network of relationships. At the center of everything we find the Restaurant, identified by a unique code and its distinctive name. Each restaurant has its own physical location in the world, and for this reason the system precisely maps its presence on the territory. Starting from the largest scale, each Province, defined by an acronym and a name, embraces within it numerous Municipalities, which are also cataloged with a specific code and name. It is within these municipalities that each address, with its street and house number, comes to life, becoming the home of one or more restaurants, thus allowing chains and franchising to be elegantly managed.

But a restaurant is above all made up of people. The system registers each Person through their tax code and surname, recognizing that behind every activity there is a human story. The ownership structure is managed with flexibility: a restaurant can have multiple owners and, vice versa, one person can hold shares in several premises. For each relationship of this type, the participation fee is recorded precisely. Furthermore, the system goes beyond the simple professional relationship, allowing you to also map the kinship ties between people. Each relationship between two individuals is defined by a specific Kinship Type, describing the nature of their bond, whether familial or otherwise.

Finally, the beating heart of every restaurant: its cuisine. Each dish, with its name and code, is a unique creation that can be offered in multiple restaurants, perhaps at a different cost depending on the venue. To ensure transparency and security, the system breaks down each dish into its individual ingredients. This relationship lets you know that an ingredient can be used in countless different recipes. But the attention to the customer is maximum: each ingredient is in turn linked to all the potential intolerances or allergies that it could trigger. In this way, it is possible to trace a clear path from a specific allergy to every single dish on the menu that contains it, offering an impeccable and safe service for every customer.
