This system was designed to orchestrate the academic world, tracing the educational path of students within the complex but fascinating university structure. At the top of this organization we find the University, the beating heart of higher education, identified by a unique code and known by its distinctive name. Each University is divided into a plurality of Departments, each in turn identified by its own code. This relationship is fundamental: each Department belongs to one and the same University, creating a clear hierarchy that organizes the educational offer and research.

The educational offer takes the form of various degree courses, each defined by a name and a specific code that guarantees its uniqueness. This is where the Student's journey begins. Once enrolled, each student receives a student number which will accompany them throughout their academic career. The system establishes a precise and direct link: each student is enrolled in a single degree course and, consequently, belongs to a single department. This allows us to know with certainty which course of study and which academic area each student belongs to, while, on the other hand, each Department and each Degree Course welcome numerous students.

Finally, to temporally contextualize this path, Registration comes into play. Each enrollment is characterized by an Academic Year, which acts as a time marker to record the student's participation in educational activities in a given period, thus completing the picture of his or her university life.
