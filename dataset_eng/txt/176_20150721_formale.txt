The data model describes an online sales system structured around five main entities. The `Seller` entity is uniquely identified by `seller_id` (PK). The `Product` and `ProductCategory` entities are defined by the `product_id` and `product_category_id` primary keys respectively. The `Advertisement` entity acts as an associative and detail entity, with a composite primary key consisting of `fk_product_category`, `fk_product` and `Lot`. This structure implements one-to-many relationships between `Seller` and `SaleAd`, between `ProductCategory` and `SaleAd`, and between `Product` and `SaleAd`, indicating that each ad is attributable to a single seller, product and category, which can be associated with multiple ads. Finally, the `Review` entity, identified by `review_id` (PK), is linked to `Product` by a one-to-many relationship, allowing a single product to receive multiple reviews.
