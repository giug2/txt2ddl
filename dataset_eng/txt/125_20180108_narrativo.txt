Welcome to our sports archive, a system designed to capture and organize the universe of team competitions. At the broadest level, the world is divided into Nations, each identified by a unique code and known by its name. Each nation is a mosaic of countless cities, each with its own identity, represented by a code and a specific name, and inextricably linked to the nation it belongs to. It is in these cities that sporting passion was born, giving life to clubs. A single city may be the proud home of multiple teams, but each club has its roots in one and only one city. Each of these companies is unique, defined not only by an identification code, but also by its glorious name, by the corporate color that dyes its shirts and by the year of foundation that marks the beginning of its history. The beating heart of every club are, of course, its Players. A club can have numerous athletes in its squad, but each player, at a given moment, dedicates his talent to just one team. Each player is registered with a personal code and described by details such as surname, first name, height and weight. To complete the picture, each athlete is assigned a specific role on the field, identified by a code and a name that describes their tactical function; although many players may share the same role, such as attacker or defender, each individual player specializes in a well-defined position.
