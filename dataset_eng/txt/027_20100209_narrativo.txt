Welcome to our banking management system, designed to put the relationship between the bank and its customers at the center. Each person who trusts us is registered as a "Customer" entity, uniquely and securely identified by their Tax Code. We store essential personal information for each customer such as the surname, name, date of birth and main address of residence. At the same time, we manage financial products, such as the "Current account", each distinguished by its own unique number. The heart of our system lies in the flexibility with which these two worlds are connected: a single customer can be the owner of multiple current accounts, and, at the same time, a single current account can be jointly held by multiple customers, allowing agile management of family or business accounts. To make this relationship even more personalized, when a customer is associated with an account, it is possible to specify a "Specific Address" for that particular user, different from the customer's primary address, ensuring that communications relating to that account are sent exactly where desired.
