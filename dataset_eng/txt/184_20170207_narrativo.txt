This system was designed to efficiently and completely manage the entire purchasing and shipping process, putting the customer experience at the center. The heart of the system is represented by our `Customer`, each person is registered with a unique `Customer Code`, together with their `Name` and `Shipping Address`. When a customer decides to make a purchase, they generate an `Order`, which is tracked with a specific `Order_ID` and contains the `Total Amount` of the transaction. The structure ensures that each order is always linked to a single customer, while a loyal customer can naturally place numerous orders over time.

Each order, however, is more than just a total; it is a collection of specific products. For this reason, each `Order` is made up of one or more `Order Details` items. Each of these rows, identified by a unique `Row_ID`, represents a single item purchased, recording its specific `Price` at the time of the transaction and the `Insertion Date`. This granularity allows us to understand exactly what each package contains. Detail entries link directly to our `Product` catalogue, where each item is defined by a unique `ProductCode` and a descriptive `ProductName`. In this way, a single product can be present in countless different orders, but each detail line always refers only to a specific product.

Finally, to complete the cycle, logistics comes into play. Once an order is ready, it is handed over to a `Courier` for delivery. Each courier in our system is identified by a `Courier Code` and its `Courier Name`. The system ensures that each individual `Order` is handled by a designated courier, allowing us to track the shipment precisely. At the same time, an efficient courier can manage the delivery of many different orders, ensuring that all packages reach their destination. Together, these elements create a coherent and traceable flow of information, from the customer's choice of product to home delivery.
