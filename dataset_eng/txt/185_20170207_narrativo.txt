This system was designed to map the intricate and fascinating world of the music industry, telling the story of how creativity takes shape, from raw talent to finished product. At the center of this universe lies the Record Label, the beating heart that discovers and promotes talent. Each label is unique, identified by its own code and characterized by its distinctive name, and serves as a home for a stable of professionals.

Under the aegis of a single record label, we find the two key figures of musical creation: the Artist and the Producer. Many artists, each with their own stage name and unique identifier, can be linked to the same label, forming a creative family. Similarly, producers, essential figures in shaping the sound, also belong to a specific record label, and multiple producers can offer their expertise under the same brand.

The creative path of an artist is expressed in his discography. Our system tracks this journey clearly: each artist can release an unlimited number of albums, but each album, with its specific title, is unmistakably the work of a single artist. This direct relationship ensures that each work is correctly attributed to its primary creator.

However, the magic of music often lies in collaboration. An album, in fact, can be the result of the collective genius of multiple figures. For this reason, the system models a flexible and dynamic relationship between producers and albums. A single producer can lend his talents to numerous different albums, crossing genres and collaborating with various artists. At the same time, a single album can benefit from the experience and vision of multiple producers, working together to create a masterpiece. In this way, the system not only stores data, but reveals the complex network of relationships and synergies that define every musical success.
