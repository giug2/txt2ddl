So basically we have a system that keeps track of some stuff. There is a sort of list with the product categories, you know, like electronics, clothes and so on, each with its own code and name to clarify. Each category of these can contain lots of different brands, and each brand has its own identifier and name. Then, a single brand obviously produces quite a few products. A product is defined by its name and the brand that makes it, so it is a combination of the two that makes it unique, and what's more it has its own specific typology. But it doesn't end there, because many pieces of a certain product model are manufactured, and each of these is an item in itself, with its serial number that never repeats and the date on which it was put together. Finally, there are the customers, registered with their ID, name and surname. The interesting thing is that each customer can be linked to quite a few of these specific items, probably the ones they have bought or registered.
