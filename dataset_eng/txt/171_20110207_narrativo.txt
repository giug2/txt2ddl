Welcome to the beating heart of our digital library, a system meticulously designed to manage the wonderful journey that each book takes, from its publication to the hands of our readers. At the center of this universe lies the **Book**, each copy unique and unmistakable thanks to its **ISBN** code. For each book, we keep essential information such as the **Title**, the **Publication Year** and, for daily management, the number of **Available Copies** ready to be discovered. To help readers navigate our extensive collection, each book is associated with a unique **Genre**, identified by a **GenreID** and described by its **GenreName**. Likewise, each volume has its own precise origin, being published by a single **Publisher**, which we recognize through its **IDEditore** and **PublisherName**. Although a publisher can publish countless works and a genre can encompass thousands of titles, each individual book uniquely belongs to one of these categories. Of course, a library lives thanks to its readers. Each **User** is registered in our system with a personal **Card** number, along with his **Full Name** and **Address**. The interaction between books and readers takes shape through **Lending**. Each time a book is checked out, a unique transaction is generated, identified by a **LoanID**, which records the **LoanDate** and current **Status** of the transaction. Each loan is always linked to a single user, although the same user can make numerous loans over time. The relationship between books and loans is dynamic and flexible: a single book can be the subject of many different loans over its lifetime, and at the same time, a single loan transaction can involve multiple books at the same time, creating a complete history of our community's readings and literary adventures.
