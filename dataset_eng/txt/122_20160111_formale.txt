The data model defines two main entities: VERSION and ACCESSORY. The VERSION entity is uniquely identified by a `version_id` attribute, which serves as the primary key. The ACCESSORY entity is identified by a `CODE` as the primary key and has a descriptive attribute `NAME`. There is a relationship with many-to-many cardinality (N:N) between the two entities, indicating that a version can be associated with multiple accessories and, vice versa, an accessory can be available for multiple versions. This association is characterized by a `price` attribute, which specifies the cost of a specific accessory in relation to a specific version.
