This system was designed to manage a highly customizable product catalog, offering total flexibility in configuring the options available to customers. At the center of this universe we find the concept of "Version", which represents the basic model of a product, such as the fitting out of a car or the edition of a software. Each single version is unequivocally identified by a unique code, the version_id, which guarantees its traceability without any possibility of error. To enrich and customize each version, the "Accessory" entity comes into play. Each accessory, which can be an option such as a sunroof or an additional function, has its own identification code for inventory management and a descriptive name that clarifies its function to the end user. The beating heart of the system lies in the dynamic relationship that links the versions to the accessories. The structure is designed to be extremely versatile: the same version can be equipped with numerous different accessories, and, at the same time, a single accessory can be compatible and available for multiple versions. The true intelligence of the model is manifested in the way the cost is managed: the price of an accessory is not a fixed value, but depends specifically on the combination between the chosen version and the accessory itself. In this way, the system is able to define a tailor-made price for each specific configuration, reflecting the value and complexity of installing a particular accessory on a specific model.
