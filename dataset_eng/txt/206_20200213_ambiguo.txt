So, the thing here is that we have Divisions, which are sort of the fulcrum of everything. Every project that starts must necessarily belong to one of these divisions, and obviously a division can have a lot of different projects under it. The same thing goes for people, employees: each of them is assigned to a specific division, and a division brings together quite a lot of people. Then, each employee has his own reference office, and multiple people can easily share the same office. The somewhat particular thing is that to identify an employee in a unique way, his personal code is not enough, but you also need to know which division and which office he is connected to, it's a mix of things. It also works in a similar way for projects, to be sure which one we are talking about, we need both its code and that of the division to which it is linked. The divisions and offices, however, are simpler, each one has its own number and that is enough to find them.
