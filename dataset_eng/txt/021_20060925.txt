Show the conceptual scheme of a database for painting exhibitions in an art gallery, according to the following 
specifications. 
• The gallery has a series of rooms, each of which has a name and size. 
• An exhibition has a title, a start date and an end date. 
• In each exhibition several paintings are exhibited and the same painting can appear in multiple exhibitions. 
• For each painting, the author, the year, and the room in which it is exhibited are represented (which can change in exhibitions 
different)
It is of interest to represent possible itineraries of an exhibition, each with a code and made up of a list 
order of rooms to visit and, for each room, an ordered list of paintings to see. 
• We also want to represent guided visits to the exhibitions which each have a name, a starting time, a 
duration and are based on a certain itinerary. The guided tours are repeated on the various days of the exhibition with a number of 
different participants
