Welcome to our system, a platform designed to organize and manage study trips in a complete and intuitive way. Imagine being able to plan every detail of your next educational adventure, from starting to earning a certificate, all in one place. Our digital world revolves around the prestigious `Colleges`, the heart of our offering. Each college is a unique entity, identified by a specific code, and has a name, physical address and telephone number to facilitate each contact.

These colleges are not remote islands, but are rooted in fascinating 'Locations' around the world. Each location, whether a vibrant metropolis or a quiet town, can host numerous colleges, thus offering a wide range of cultural and academic choices. Of course, each individual college belongs to one and only one location, thus ensuring a clear geographic association. To reach these destinations, the system flexibly manages the logistics of the trip. We start from a specific `Departure City`, which in turn is connected to one or more `Airports`. This flexibility allows us to recognize that a large city can be served by multiple airports and, vice versa, an airport can be a starting point for travelers coming from different cities. Each airport, identified by a code and name, is clearly associated with the destination `Location`, ensuring that your arrival is always in the right place.

Once you've chosen your destination and college, it's time to define your study experience. Each college offers different `Accommodation` packages, each characterized by a specific `Period`, a `Price` and a number of `Hours` of lessons. These stay options are unique to each institution, allowing for a personalized offering. To enrich the training path, it is possible to take an 'Exam' to certify the skills acquired. Colleges provide a catalog of exams, each with its own name and an associated 'Cost', inextricably linked to the institution that provides them. Finally, to make these opportunities even more accessible, the system manages different types of discounts. A `College` may offer more than one `Type of Discount`, such as early booking or group discounts, and at the same time, a certain type of discount may be available at multiple participating colleges, creating an interconnected and advantageous ecosystem of benefits for the student.
