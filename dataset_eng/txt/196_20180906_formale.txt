The data model describes a healthcare and administrative management system. The `ASL` entity, identified by a unique `ASL Code` and a `Name`, manages one or more `Hospital` entities. Each `Hospital` is identified by a `Hospital Code` and a `Structure Name` and belongs to only one `ASL`. The `Patient` entity, identified by the `Fiscal Code` as the primary key (PK) and characterized by `Surname`, `Name` and `BirthDate`, can be associated with multiple `Admissions`. Each `Hospital` is uniquely identified by a `Hospital_ID`, specifies a `Department` and refers to a single `Patient` and a single `Hospital`. Each `Admission` can be associated with one or more `Invoices`, each identified by an `Invoice Number` (PK) and containing `Issue Date` and `Amount`. Finally, each `Invoice` can be paid via one or more `Payment`, where each transaction is identified by a `Transaction_ID` (PK) and reports `Payment Data` and `Paid Amount`.
