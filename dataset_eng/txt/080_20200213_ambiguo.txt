So, in practice there is this large structure, the Faculty, which has its own code to be recognized. Within each Faculty, then, you will find a few Departments; each department is clearly linked to a single Faculty, it cannot be in the middle. Indeed, to identify a Department its code is not enough, you must also know which Faculty it belongs to, it is a kind of pair of information. In cascade, each Department has a lot of people who work there, the Teachers, and each of them is assigned to a single Department. And also for teachers, to know who is who, you need their personal code and that of the department they belong to. Same story for the Study Courses: there are several for each Department, but each course belongs to only one department. In short, everything is connected in a hierarchical way, with the departments acting as the central hub between the faculties and the people or courses, and to know exactly what or who you are talking about, you always have to also know the piece above.
