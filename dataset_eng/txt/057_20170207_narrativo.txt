This system was designed to tell the story of every work initiative within a company, harmoniously managing people, their teams, the projects they dedicate themselves to and the clients who commissioned them. At the center of this universe we find the **Clients**, our external partners, each uniquely identified. They are the ones who start everything, entrusting us with the **Projects**. Each project is born inextricably linked to its client; in fact, a project is not only defined by its identification code, but also by the identity of the client who requested it, creating an inseparable bond. This means that while a single client can be the source of numerous projects, each project always belongs to a single client. Within the company, the structure is organized into **Departments**, which represent the various areas of expertise and are also individually identified. It is within these departments that **Employees** operate. The structure emphasizes that an employee is an integral part of his department, so much so that his identity in the system is given by the combination of his personal code and the department to which he belongs. Naturally, a department has many employees within it. The real beating heart of the system is the interaction between people and work: an employee can make his skills available on multiple projects at the same time, and, in the same way, a single project requires the collaboration of a team made up of several employees, creating a dynamic network of collaborations that allows resources to be allocated flexibly and efficiently.
