Welcome to the beating heart of an airline system, a digital universe where every journey, from the smallest to the largest, is meticulously planned and tracked. Our story begins with the map of the world, defined by the different NATIONS, each with its own identifying code and name. Within these nations, there are countless CITIES, which represent the urban centers and destinations of our travellers. The true starting and ending point of every aviation adventure is the AIRPORT, a physical portal identified by a unique code and located in a specific city, which can host more than one airport to manage the flow of passengers.

What gives life to these connections are the airlines, each with its own identity and a nation to which it belongs. They are the ones who orchestrate the FLIGHTS, which represent the scheduled and recurring routes. A flight is not just a number, but is an entity uniquely defined by the combination of its number and the company that operates it. Each flight is a bridge between two airports: one for departure and one for arrival. Its regularity is established by a calendar that associates it with the specific DAYS of the week in which it operates, creating a reliable timetable. Additionally, for each planned route, a specific AIRCRAFT_TYPE is designated, such as a Boeing 737 or an Airbus A320, ensuring that the right aircraft is always scheduled for the right trip.

Of course, an aircraft type is a concept, a model. Reality is made of concrete machines: AIRCRAFT, i.e. individual physical aircraft, each with its own registration code, which belong to one of the aircraft type categories. This is where plan meets reality. A planned FLIGHT turns into an ACTUAL FLIGHT on a specific DATE. This concrete event represents the realization of the journey: for each actual flight, we precisely record the time of ACTUAL DEPARTURE and ACTUAL ARRIVAL. And, crucially, each actual flight is assigned a specific AIRCRAFT from the fleet to fly that route, thus connecting the abstract plan to the physical machine that will soar through the skies, completing the journey cycle.
