This system was designed to manage the operational dynamics of a company in an integrated way, orchestrating the people, customers and projects they work on. At the center of everything we find our precious Customers, each registered with a unique identifier, `customer_id`, and characterized by a name and surname. The relationship with customers takes shape through the projects commissioned to us. Each Project, identified by a `project_id` and defined by a `project_name` and a `start_date`, is inextricably linked to a single Customer, as indicated by its `fk_customer`. This structure makes it clear that a client may have numerous projects under its belt, but each individual project belongs exclusively to a single client.

Our internal team, organized into Departments, brings these projects to life. Each Department has its own unique code, `department_id`, and a specific name that describes its function, such as `department_name`. The Employees work within this structure. Each Employee is identified by their `employee_id`, has a first and last name, and is assigned to a unique and specific department via the `fk_department`. This means that while a department is made up of many employees, each employee is part of only one team.

The interaction between these components is what defines the workflow. The system is designed to be flexible: a single Employee can collaborate with multiple Customers over time, and, similarly, a Customer can be followed by several members of our team, creating a dynamic network of relationships. This flexibility also extends to project management: multiple Employees can join forces to work on a single Project, and a talented Employee can contribute his or her skills to multiple projects simultaneously, thus ensuring optimal resource allocation and effective collaboration to achieve set goals.
