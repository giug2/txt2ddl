This system was designed to tell the complete story of each purchase interaction, putting the figure of our customer at the center. It all begins with the Customer, a unique person identified by his specific Customer Code, whose Name and Surname we know. Our relationship with him is built over time through the purchases he decides to make. Every time a customer makes a purchase, they generate a new Order. A single customer can create an unlimited number of orders over time, but each order is unambiguously and permanently tied to just one customer, thus creating a clear history of their choices. Each order is in turn a unique event, marked by its own OrderID and the OrderDate on which it was placed. However, an order is much more than just a date; its true value lies in the products it contains. For this reason, each order is divided into one or more Order Lines, which represent its beating heart. A single line describes a specific product purchased within that order and, to be unambiguously identified, uses a combination of the reference to the order to which it belongs and a progressive LineNumber. Within each line we find all the essential details: the Product Description, its Price, the expected Delivery Date and even the name of the Supplier from which the item comes. This allows us to not only know what was purchased, but also when it will arrive and who supplied it, offering a complete and detailed view of every single transaction, from the general customer to the smallest detail of the product.
