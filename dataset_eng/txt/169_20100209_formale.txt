The data model describes human resource management on company projects. The `Project` entity is uniquely identified by the `ProjectCode` attribute, which acts as the primary key. The `Employee` entity represents the registered personnel and is identified by the `Serial number` (PK); it also contains the descriptive attributes `Surname`, `First Name`, `Employment Date` and `Main Role`. There is a many-to-many (N:N) cardinality relationship between the entities `Project` and `Employee`, indicating that an employee can participate in multiple projects and, vice versa, a project can involve multiple employees. This association is characterized by the `RoleInProject` attribute, which specifies the employee's function in the context of a specific project to which he or she is assigned.
