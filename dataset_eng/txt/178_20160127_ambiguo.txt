So basically we have a few things to keep in order. First of all there are the Students, each with their own student number which is the thing that identifies them uniquely, no two are the same. Then obviously we know the name of a student, where he lives and what year of the course he arrived at. Then there are the Degree Courses, and these also have their own specific code to recognize them, in addition to the full name of the course. Finally, we have the Departments, which are the largest containers, and they also have an identification code and a name. The logic is that a Department can have quite a few Degree Courses under it, but a single course belongs only to that Department. In turn, each degree course is attended by a lot of students, while a student is enrolled in only one course at a time and cannot do multiple things at the same time.
