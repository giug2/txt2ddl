- each manager works in one and only one department and collaborates with one or more assistants
• one or more managers work in each department and all and only the assistants who collaborate with these managers belong to it
• each assistant collaborates with one and only one manager and belongs to the department in which that manager works
• each assistant and each manager each have one and only one location, but the location of an assistant does not necessarily coincide with that of the relevant manager;
-for each location there can be multiple assistants and multiple managers
• note that the concept of department is virtual and we are not talking about the headquarters of a department
