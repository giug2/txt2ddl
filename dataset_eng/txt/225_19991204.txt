The scheme describes a domain in which the Brands are managed, each uniquely identified by a Brand Code and characterized by a Name and a Country of Origin. Each brand markets Products, which are defined by an association with the Brand they belong to, a Product Name and a Type. The system also tracks individual Items in stock, each identified by its own unique Serial Number. Each item, attributable to a specific product, has a Production Year and a buyer. The latter is connected to the Customer entity, where each individual is identified by their Customer Code and described by Surname and Name.
