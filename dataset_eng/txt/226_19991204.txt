The scheme outlines an information system for an e-commerce in which there are Product Categories, each identified by a Category Code and described by a Category Name. The Brands are connected to these, defined by an identifying BrandID, a BrandName and the category to which they belong, establishing a referential integrity constraint with the CategoriaMerceologica entity. Each brand in turn produces Products, which are identified by the pair of attributes Brand and ProductName and characterized by a Type; the Brand attribute creates a referential link with the respective Brand. The individual Items in stock are recorded using a unique Serial Number and can be traced back to a specific Product. Each Item is also described by a Production Data and is associated with a buyer, whose code refers to the Customers entity. The latter collects the personal information of customers, identified by their Customer ID and described by Surname and Name.
