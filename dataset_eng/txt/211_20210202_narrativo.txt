Welcome to the heart of our academic management system, a structure designed to follow the educational path of each student within the university step by step. The organization starts from the broadest level: the Departments. Each Department, such as 'Mathematical, Physical and Natural Sciences', is identified by a unique code and a specific name that defines its area of ​​expertise. The various degree courses flourish under the aegis of each Department. A single Department can offer multiple study paths, but each course - which is distinguished by its own code, name and teaching location - belongs unequivocally to a single Department. The protagonists of this world are the Students. Each of them, uniquely identified by their Student Number and registered with their name, enrolls in a single and specific Degree Course, thus populating the virtual and physical classrooms of our university. Naturally, a Degree Course welcomes numerous students within it, creating a learning community. The academic progress of each student is meticulously tracked through the exams taken. A student's career is a sequence of exams, and our system reflects this reality: a single student can take and record many exams over time. Each exam passed is a unique event, identified by a specific registration ID in combination with the student number who took it, guaranteeing a direct and unmistakable association. For each of these tests, we carefully record the date on which it took place and the grade achieved, thus building a complete history of the success of each of our members.
