This system was designed to orchestrate the organization of study trips and training trips abroad in every detail, offering a complete vision of the entire experience. The journey begins with the choice of a departure city, identified by a unique code and name, and the logistics of the flight. The system manages a network of airports, each with its own code and name, and allows great flexibility: from a single departure city you can fly from multiple airports, and each airport can serve several cities, thus guaranteeing the best possible travel combination.

Once the flight has been defined, attention shifts to the final destination, i.e. the study location. Each location is connected to a home airport, which serves as a gateway for students. It is in these locations that the heart of the educational experience takes place. In fact, each of them hosts one or more colleges, which represent the nerve centers of training. Each college is carefully registered with an identification code, name, full address, telephone number and the number of hours of lessons offered.

To further enrich the academic path, the system keeps track of the exams that can be taken in each location, specifying their name and associated cost, thus allowing students to also plan to obtain certifications. In addition to studying, accommodation is fundamental: each college offers different accommodation options, each defined by a specific period and price, to satisfy different needs in terms of duration and budget. Finally, to make the offer even more advantageous, the system manages a catalog of reduction types. Thanks to a flexible mechanism, each college can be associated with multiple types of discounts and, at the same time, a single reduction can be applied to several colleges, allowing the creation of personalized promotions and targeted campaigns.
