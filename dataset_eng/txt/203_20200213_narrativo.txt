Imagine a digital ecosystem designed to map and manage the complex world of software development, where every element has its place and every connection tells a story of collaboration and progress. At the center of this universe we find the Company, the fundamental entity identified by a unique code and its name, which acts as a container for all activities and resources. Each Company gives life to multiple Projects, each with its own name and a unique identifier which, to avoid ambiguity, is always associated with the parent company. This link ensures that a project belongs unambiguously to one organization.

The beating heart of every company is the people: the Developers. Each developer, characterized by name and surname, is a member of a specific company and is part of a well-defined team. To ensure perfect traceability, a developer's identity is determined by a unique combination of his personal code, the team he belongs to and the company he works for. This structure creates a clear hierarchy: multiple developers can be part of a single Team, and each team, in turn, is a cohesive unit with its own name and identifier.

The real magic of this system, however, lies in the way in which skills are put to use. The relationship between Developers and Projects is flexible and dynamic: a single developer can lend his talents to multiple projects simultaneously, and, conversely, a complex project is typically the result of the joint effort of numerous developers. This network of collaborations allows you to precisely track who is working on what, offering a complete and interconnected view that links individuals to teams, teams to projects, and the entire production effort to the company that leads it.
