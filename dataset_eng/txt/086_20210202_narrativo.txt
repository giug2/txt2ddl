This system was designed to efficiently track and manage health information at a territorial level, creating a clear information network that connects citizens to health facilities and diagnostic test results. At the top of our organizational structure we find the Region, each single geographical and administrative entity uniquely identified by a Region ID code and characterized by its own Name. Within each Region, the health service is divided into various ASLs, the Local Health Authorities. Each ASL belongs to one and only one Region, and to guarantee its univocal identification throughout the national territory, it is registered not only with its own CodASL but also with a direct reference to the region it belongs to; the Name and Address of the office complete your profile. The ASLs represent the direct point of contact with citizens, in fact each Patient in the system is associated with a specific ASL of competence. A Patient, identified unequivocally by his Fiscal Code (CF) and registered with his Name, can be cared for by only one ASL, while each ASL takes care of numerous patients. Finally, to monitor the state of health, each Patient can undergo one or more swabs over time. Each swab is a unique event, characterized by a CT identification code, and records crucial information such as the execution date and the test result. This structure guarantees that every single swab can always be traced back to a single patient, allowing us to build a complete and reliable history of the tests carried out by each individual.
