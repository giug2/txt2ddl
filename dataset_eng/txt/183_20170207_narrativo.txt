This system was designed to tell the story of each treatment path within a healthcare facility, putting people and the care they receive at the centre. The protagonist of each story is the Patient, each individual is uniquely registered with his own identifier, together with his name and surname, to ensure that all information is always associated with the correct person. Our team of Doctors take care of patients, each of whom is identified not only by their name, surname and a personal code, but also by the Operating Unit to which they belong. This allows us to know that each doctor works within a specific department, such as cardiology or paediatrics, and that each Operating Unit, in turn, brings together numerous professionals. A patient's treatment path takes shape through Treatments: every single therapy or intervention is recorded with its own code, a specific name and the start date. Fundamentally, each treatment is inextricably linked to a single patient, allowing us to build a complete and accurate medical history for each person we care for, who may naturally undergo multiple treatments over time. Modern healthcare is a team effort, and our system reflects this collaborative reality. The relationship between Doctors and Patients is flexible: a doctor can follow many patients, and a single patient can be entrusted to the care of multiple specialists from different disciplines. Likewise, treatment management is a dynamic process: a complex treatment may require the intervention of multiple doctors and, vice versa, a single doctor is involved in the administration of numerous treatments for the different patients he/she has in charge, creating a complete map of the human and professional interactions that define healthcare.
