So, basically here we have a few things that revolve around the university. There is a list of teachers, each with their own personal code to recognize them. Then there are the courses, and here the thing is a bit particular: each course is identified not only by its own ID but also by that of the professor who teaches it, so they are closely linked. This means that a professor can have a lot of courses, but one course belongs to that professor and that's it. Obviously there are also the students, each with their own number, and the various departments, such as physics or literature, also with a code. As for connections, a student can enroll in a bunch of different courses, and at the same time a course can have an infinite number of students, there's a lot of comings and goings. Finally, each course belongs to only one department, while a department, of course, has quite a few courses under it.
