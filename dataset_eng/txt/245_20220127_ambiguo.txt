Basically, we keep track of a few things about hospitals here. First of all, we have a list of hospitals, each with its own code to avoid confusion, and obviously we know where they are, with the address and city. Each hospital is then divided into various departments; a department has its own name, like "Cardiology", and we know which floor it is on. The important thing is that a department is linked to a single hospital, it cannot exist alone or in multiple places at the same time. Then, of course, there is the staff: we have the doctors and nurses. For each of them we wrote down a personal identifier, in addition to the name and surname, so we know who is who. The slightly more tangled part is how these people work together in the departments: essentially, a lot of doctors, a lot of nurses and a lot of departments are all connected together in some kind of big network. There is no fixed rule, a doctor can work with many different nurses and perhaps they do it in multiple departments, it's all very flexible and interconnected.
