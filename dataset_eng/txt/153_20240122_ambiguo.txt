So basically here we have a system that tracks some college stuff. There are faculties, and each has its own code to recognize it. Then, each faculty is connected to a lot of different courses, but a specific course always belongs only to one faculty, it cannot be in the middle. Besides this, there are the professors and the students, and they also have their own personal identifier. It gets a little messy when we look at how they mix together: one professor can teach a bunch of different courses, and at the same time a single course can be taught by multiple professors. Same story for students: a student enrolls in as many courses as he wants, and obviously every course is full of students. Consequently, there is also a bond between professors and students, where a professor has to deal with many kids and each kid, in his journey, meets quite a few different professors. In short, it's all a great intersection of people and materials.
