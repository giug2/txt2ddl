Welcome to the beating heart of our musical universe, a system designed to capture the essence and history of music festivals around the world. At the center of this galaxy of sounds and performances lies the **Festival** entity, each instance of which represents a unique and unrepeatable event. Each festival is identified by a unique code, `Festival_ID`, and defined by its specific `Name` and `EditionYear`, allowing us to distinguish, for example, the 2023 edition of a famous event from its 2024 incarnation.

Of course, every big event needs a stage. This is where **Location** comes into play, the physical place that hosts the magic. Each location is registered with its own unique `Location_ID` and is described by the `CityName` and the `Country` in which it is located. The link between these two worlds is fundamental: a single location, such as an iconic city, can be the cradle of numerous different festivals over the years, but each specific edition of a festival takes place in one and only one location, creating an indissoluble bond between the event and its territory.

What would a festival be without its protagonists? **Artists** are the stars that light up our stages. Each artist in our system is its own entity, with an `Artist_ID` that uniquely identifies it. We know his `Art Name`, by which he is famous to the public, but we also keep his `Real Name` and `Career Start Year` to trace his professional path. The relationship with the festival is vibrant and direct: each edition of a festival hosts a line-up of many artists, creating a precise chronology of who played and when.

Finally, to give a soul and color to music, we have the **Musical Genre**. Each genre, from rock to electronic, is cataloged with a unique `Genre_ID` and its `Name`. This allows us to weave another important connection: each artist is associated with a main musical genre that defines his style. While a genre can obviously include countless artists, in our system each artist is primarily linked to a single genre, helping us map the soundscape of each event and understand the musical currents that drive our festivals.
