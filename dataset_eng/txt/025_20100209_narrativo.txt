This system was designed to clearly and securely manage a bank's fundamental information, putting the relationship between people and their financial instruments at the centre. At the heart of the system we find the **Customer**, the central figure of every operation. To ensure that each individual is recognized in a unique and unmistakable way, each customer is registered through his **Fiscal Code**, which serves as the primary identification key. In addition to this essential data, the system stores complete personal information such as **Surname**, **Name**, **Date of Birth** and **Address** of residence, thus creating a detailed and reliable profile for each person. At the same time, the system manages the main banking product: the **Current Account**. Each account is in turn a unique entity, identified by a specific **Number** that distinguishes it from all the others. The real strength of this structure lies in the link that unites these two realities: it has been envisaged that a single **Customer** can be the owner of multiple current accounts, to satisfy different needs for managing their money. At the same time, to ensure maximum transparency and responsibility, each **Current Account** must be linked to a single customer. In this way, the system always guarantees precise and secure mapping, knowing exactly who owns each individual account and which accounts are associated with each customer.
