Welcome to the information system that orchestrates academic life, a digital framework designed to map and manage the fundamental interactions between key components of our institution. At the center of everything we find the Departments, the large thematic areas that act as an organizational pillar for the training offer; each department is unique and acts as a container for the disciplines within its competence. Individual courses are created and managed within each Department. Each course is unequivocally linked to a single department, thus guaranteeing a clear academic position, although a department can obviously provide a wide variety of courses. The soul of each course is the teacher: our data structure underlines this indissoluble bond, associating each specific course with a single responsible teacher. This teacher, in turn, can hold multiple courses, making use of his experience in different subjects. Finally, it is the Students who give life to this structure, each identified by a personal identification number. Their training path unfolds through enrollment in various courses. The flexibility of the system allows each student to follow multiple courses at the same time, and, conversely, each course is designed to accommodate a plurality of students, thus creating a dynamic and interconnected learning community. In summary, the system models a network in which a department offers many courses, each course is taught by a single teacher and attended by many students, who in turn build their own educational background by choosing from the many courses available.
