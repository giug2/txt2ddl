This system was designed to tell the complete story of each student's academic journey within a university. At the top of the organizational structure we find the Department, identified by a unique Code and a Name, which represents the large house of the different areas of knowledge. Each Department creates one or more specific training courses, known as Degree Courses, each characterized by its own identification code and name. The relationship is clear: a single Department can offer many Degree Courses, but each Degree Course belongs unequivocally to only one Department. It is in this context that the protagonist of our story makes his entrance: the Student. Each student is a unique person, defined by a Name and Surname, but in the system he is recognized unmistakably by his Student Number. Upon enrolment, the student enrolls in a single Degree Course, creating a direct link that also associates him with the single Department he belongs to. Although a degree course welcomes numerous students, each of them is enrolled in only one course at a time. The academic journey unfolds through the study of different subjects, the Teachings, also defined by a unique Code and a Name. The relationship between students and courses is the most dynamic: over the course of his career, a student can take exams for many different courses, and, at the same time, each course is attended by a multitude of students. The culmination of this interaction is the recording of the Grade, an attribute that belongs neither to the student alone nor to the teaching alone, but which exists solely to certify the result obtained by a specific student in a specific exam, thus weaving the detailed plot of his academic success.
