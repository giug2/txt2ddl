This system was designed to navigate and map the vast and fascinating universe of literature, creating a network of connections between creative minds, their works, the worlds they contain and those who bring them to light. At the center of everything we find the fundamental figure of the **Author**, a unique and distinct entity that represents the point of origin of every story. Each author in our system is the creator of one or more **Novels**, thus establishing a deep and direct connection: while a single author can give life to an entire library of works, each novel is unequivocally the fruit of the genius of a single and specific author. Once a novel takes shape, the **Publisher** comes into play, the publishing house that is responsible for transforming the manuscript into a book for the public. A publisher may have a catalog full of countless novels, but each individual work is published under the auspices of only one publisher. But the beating heart of every novel lies in its **Characters**, the figures who animate the pages and live the adventures. The relationship here is the most dynamic and complex: a single novel can be the stage for a multitude of different characters, and, at the same time, an iconic character is not necessarily confined to a single story, being able to reappear in sequels, sagas or entire series, thus weaving a narrative thread that runs through multiple novels. In this way, the system doesn't just catalog, but tells the story behind the story, connecting the author to his creation, the creation to his publisher, and the work to his universe of unforgettable characters.
