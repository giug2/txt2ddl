Basically, we have a system with people selling stuff. Each of them has its own identification code and can create a lot of sales ads. Each advert is a thing in itself, defined in a slightly strange way: to understand, you look at what product it refers to, what category it belongs to and a sort of lot or batch number. In short, these three pieces together make the announcement unique. Clearly, each advert is linked to only one seller. The products, in turn, are grouped into broader categories; a category can contain a lot of different ads, but an ad is always and only within one category. The same applies to the specific product: a certain item may be offered for sale in several advertisements, but each advertisement concerns exactly one item only. Finally, people can leave comments and reviews, but only on products. A product may have a bunch of reviews, but each review only ever refers to that single product.
