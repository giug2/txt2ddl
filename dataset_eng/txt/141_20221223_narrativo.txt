This system was designed to map and manage the universe of academic research, creating a network of information that connects institutions, people and places. At the center of everything we find the research INSTITUTE, each unique entity identified by a specific CODE, of which we know the NAME and YEAR OF FOUNDATION, which testifies to its history and prestige. The geographical location of each institute is fundamental: each of them is based in one and only one CITY, establishing a precise link with the territory. In turn, each CITY, also defined by its own CODE and NAME, belongs unequivocally to a single NATION, thus creating a clear hierarchy that allows navigation from the national to the urban context. Naturally, an institute is made up of the people who work there; for this reason, the system registers every single RESEARCHER, identified by a personal CODE and described by SURNAME, NAME and DATE OF BIRTH. The professional link is strong and clear: an institute may have a team made up of many researchers, but each researcher is affiliated with one and only one institute. To complete the professional profile, each researcher is assigned a specific QUALIFICATION, such as "Senior Researcher" or "Associate Professor", identified by a CODE and a NAME. While many researchers may share the same qualification, each individual holds only one, thus defining his or her role within the scientific community.
