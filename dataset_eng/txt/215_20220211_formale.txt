The data model describes the structure of a university teaching management system. The `Department` entity, uniquely identified by its `Acronym` and described by a `Name`, manages a plurality of degree courses and is staffed by multiple teachers. The `Teacher` entity is identified by the `Number` and has the `NameSurname` attribute. The `CorsoDiLaurea` entity, identified by a `Code` and a `Name`, belongs to a single department and is divided into multiple courses. Each `Course`, defined by a unique `Code` and a `Name`, is part of a single degree course and includes a series of lessons. The `Lesson` entity is identified by a `Code` and characterized by `Title` and `Duration`; each lesson belongs to a single teaching and can have one or more teaching materials associated with it. The `Didactic Material`, identified by a `Code` and described by `FileName` and `DataLoading`, is linked to a single lesson, uploaded by a single teacher and characterized by a single format. Finally, the `Format` entity is defined by a unique `ID` and an `Extension` that specifies its type.
