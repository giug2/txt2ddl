Welcome to the heart of our organizational management system, a tool designed to map and understand the complex structure of our ever-evolving company. At the center of everything are the fundamental pillars of our organization: the different offices and the specialized departments that populate them. Each Headquarters is a unique entity, a geographical reference point for our operations, just as each Department represents a core of specific skills and functions.

In this ecosystem, the figure of the Manager is absolutely central. A manager is not simply defined by his name, but by his specific role within the structure: his identity is in fact uniquely determined by the combination of the department he manages and the location in which he operates. This ensures a clear attribution of responsibility. Although each manager has a primary location, the system recognizes the dynamic and transversal nature of his work, allowing a single manager to be associated with multiple departments and, at the same time, to have multiple managers collaborating within the same department, reflecting full operational flexibility.

To support these key figures, we find the Assistants. Each assistant is tied directly and unambiguously to a single manager, creating a clear line of support and collaboration. Again, the link is not abstract, but well contextualized: an assistant is assigned to a specific manager within a specific location, ensuring that support is always localized and relevant. In this way, while a manager can rely on the contribution of numerous assistants, each assistant has a single managerial point of reference, ensuring efficiency and clarity in the chain of command.
