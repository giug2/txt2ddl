This system was designed to map the intricate and fascinating organization of a university, offering a complete vision of its academic world, from the supporting structures to the people who animate them. At the top of the organization we find the Faculties, each identified by a unique code and a specific name, which represent the major areas of knowledge. The Departments operate within this framework, real nerve centers of research and teaching, each with its own identification code, a name and a physical location. The Departments create the various Degree Courses, also recognized by a code and a name, which represent the training courses offered to students. The relationship between these entities is flexible and dynamic: a Department can manage multiple Degree Courses and, in the same way, a single Degree Course can arise from the collaboration of multiple Departments, favoring an interdisciplinary approach.

Going into the detail of the training offer, each Degree Course is divided into a series of specific teachings. Each Course, characterized by its own code and name, is strictly linked to a single reference Faculty, ensuring coherence in the organization, and at the same time is an integral part of a single and specific Degree Course, thus outlining a well-defined study path for students.

The beating heart of this structure is represented by the teachers. Each professor is registered in the system with a unique serial number, name and surname. The teachers belong to the Departments, and the system allows a teacher to be associated with multiple Departments, reflecting his versatility and transversal skills. In addition to purely academic data, the system is enriched with geographical information thanks to the City entity, which catalogs locations with a code and a name. This allows us to get to know our teaching staff better: for each professor, in fact, we can know not only in which city he currently resides, but also what his city of birth is, recognizing that multiple teachers can obviously share the same city of residence or origin.
