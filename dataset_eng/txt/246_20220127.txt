• each professor belongs to one and only one department and supervises one or more doctoral students
• each department has one or more professors and all and only the doctoral students who are supervised by these professors belong to it
• each PhD student is supervised by one and only one professor and his/her department is that of that professor
• each doctoral student and each professor each have one and only one reference campus, but the campus of a doctoral student does not necessarily coincide with that of the relevant supervising professor; for each campus there can be more doctoral students and more professors
• note that the concept of department is logical and we are not talking about a campus of a department
