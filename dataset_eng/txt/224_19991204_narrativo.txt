Welcome to our digital archive, a universe created to explore and connect the wonders of the art world. This system is designed to tell the stories behind each masterpiece, starting from their creators. At the center of everything we find the entity of the Artist, a unique figure identified by an Artist ID code and known through his Name and Surname. Each artist gives life to one or more creations, the Works of Art, which constitute the beating heart of our collection. Each OperaArte is in turn a unique piece, characterized by its own IDOpera code, a Title that evokes its essence and a Style that places it in a specific historical and cultural context. The bond between those who create and what is created is profound and sometimes complex: our system recognizes that a single Artist can be the author of countless works, but at the same time a single Work of Art can arise from the collaborative genius of multiple artists. These precious testimonies of human creativity then find their home in Museums, places of culture uniquely identified by their Name and located in a specific City. The journey of a work does not end with its creation; in fact, each Artwork, at a given moment, can be exhibited and admired within one and only Museum, while each Museum is proud to host and preserve a vast collection of different masterpieces. To enrich the visitor's experience and trace the history of each piece, when a work becomes part of a museum collection, we precisely record the Exhibition Room in which it can be admired and the Acquisition Year, which marks the moment in which that treasure became the museum's heritage. In this way, we intertwine the lives of the artists, the destinies of their works and the history of the places that preserve them for eternity.
