Welcome to the beating heart of our organization, a system designed to precisely map and manage our human and logistical resources. At the center of everything we find our physical foundations: the Headquarters. Each location is a unique point of reference, identified by a specific code and characterized by its address and the city in which it is located. Within each of these locations, working life takes place in a variety of offices. An office is not just a room, but a defined space, identified by its own code which, to be unequivocal, is always linked to the location that hosts it; we also know its specific name and the plane in which it is located. This structure ensures that each individual office belongs exclusively to one and only one location, creating a clear spatial hierarchy. Populating these spaces are the people who guide and support the company: the Managers and Assistants. For each manager and each assistant, the system records essential personal information such as name and surname, associating them with a unique identifier. However, the real power of this model lies in the way it connects people and places. The system doesn't just say who works where, but models the complex dynamics of collaboration. In fact, a flexible relationship is traced that simultaneously links a manager, an assistant and an office. This means that we can know exactly which assistant supports a specific manager and in which specific office this collaboration takes place. With this interconnected view, one executive can employ multiple assistants, one assistant can support multiple executives, and these synergies can be assigned to different offices, offering a comprehensive and dynamic mapping of responsibilities and resource allocation across the company.
