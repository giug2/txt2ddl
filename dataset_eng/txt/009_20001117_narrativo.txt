This system was designed to manage all information relating to residential complexes in a complete and organized way, offering a clear vision of the real estate structure and the people who live there. At the center of everything we find the Condominium, our starting point, uniquely identified by a specific Name and precisely located through its Address. Each condominium is a complex reality, which is divided into one or more scales for better organization; each Staircase is recognizable thanks to its own Code, but its identity is inextricably linked to the Condominium to which it belongs, thus creating a unique identification. Continuing along this detailed path, each Staircase gives access to different Apartments. An Apartment is defined by its Extension number, which makes it unique within its specific Scale. Furthermore, each apartment is associated with a value of thousandths, which is fundamental for the distribution of common expenses. Of course, these spaces are not empty, but are lived in by People. Each individual in our system is registered with their personal data, Name and Surname, and is unequivocally identified by their Tax Code. The real heart of the system lies in the way it connects properties to people, clearly defining roles. A single Apartment can have multiple People associated with it as owners, allowing co-ownership situations to be managed correctly. At the same time, the same Apartment can be inhabited by one or more Persons who appear as tenants, thus clearly distinguishing legal ownership from actual residence. This structure allows us to always have a complete and detailed picture, knowing exactly who owns and who lives in each single real estate unit within a given building.
