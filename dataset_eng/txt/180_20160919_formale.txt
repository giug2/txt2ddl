The data model defines the entities ARTIST, SONG, FESTIVAL and MUSIC_GENRE, each uniquely identified by a primary key: artist_id, song_id, festival_id and music_genre id. The PERFORMANCE entity represents a specific performance and is identified by a composite primary key, made up of the foreign keys fk_festival and fk_genre_musicale. This structure establishes that a FESTIVAL can host multiple PERFORMANCES (1:N relationship) and that each PERFORMANCE is uniquely associated with only one MUSIC_GENRE (1:1 relationship). Furthermore, a single PERFORMANCE is related to many SONGS (1:N relationship). The model also includes a one-to-many relationship between ARTIST and MUSIC_GENRE, allowing an artist to be associated with multiple music genres.
