This system was designed to flexibly map and manage the complex organizational structure of a modern company, describing the interconnections between people, operational units and their physical locations. At the heart of our organization are the physical locations, each uniquely identified; each location can host numerous departments, but each individual department is unequivocally linked to a single location, creating a clear physical link for each operational unit. The management of these departments is entrusted to managers. The system recognizes that the role of a manager can be transversal; thus, a single manager can be responsible for overseeing multiple departments at the same time and, similarly, a department, especially a large or strategic one, can benefit from the joint leadership of multiple managers. To support operational efficiency, we find the figure of assistants, whose collaboration with managers is extremely dynamic: an assistant can provide support to several managers and, vice versa, a manager can avail himself of the help of a team of multiple assistants to manage his responsibilities. Finally, to ensure complete coverage, the system also directly connects assistants to departments; this means that one assistant can be assigned to work for multiple departments, offering flexible support where it is needed most, while, in parallel, each department can rely on a team of dedicated assistants to support its daily activities.
