Welcome to our system, a digital universe designed to track the complete history of every vehicle, from its conception to its final owner. At the center of this world are people, each uniquely identified by their tax code, next to which we carefully record their name and surname. The life of a car in our system really begins with purchase, a crucial moment that binds a person to a vehicle. Each purchase transaction is a unique event, defined by the date it occurs and the specific car involved; a single person can make numerous purchases over time, thus building their automotive history, and each purchase record can be associated with multiple cars.

Each car is a distinct entity, recognizable by its license plate, and for which we keep the year of production. But a car is much more than this: it is the physical example of a specific version. Each version, characterized by details such as the engine capacity, is defined by a unique code within the model to which it belongs. A model, such as a sedan or an SUV, is in turn identified by its name and the manufacturer that created it. Of course, an automaker can produce a wide variety of models, and each model can exist in numerous different versions. Going further up the chain, each manufacturer, recognized by its own code and name, has its roots in a nation, defined by an acronym and a name. Our system is designed to manage the complexity of the global market, allowing a single manufacturer to be connected to multiple nations, and each nation to be the cradle of multiple automotive brands, creating a detailed and interconnected map of the entire sector.
