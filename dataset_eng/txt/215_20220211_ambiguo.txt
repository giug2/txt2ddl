So, in practice there is this structure where everything starts from the Departments, which we recognize by an acronym and have a complete name. Under each department you will find a number of degree courses and also a group of teachers, who are identified by a number and have their name and surname. Each Degree Course, which has its own code so as not to confuse it and a name, is made up of a lot of Teachings. In turn, each Teaching, also with a code and a name, is broken up into many Lessons. These Lessons have their own code, a title to understand what we are talking about and a duration. For each lesson, then, you can find different teaching materials. This material, which we recognize by a code, the file name and when it was uploaded, is related to a lot of things. Obviously it's linked to the specific lesson, but it's also uploaded by a certain Teacher, so we know who put it there. And then, each piece of material has its own format, such as PDF, video, in short, which is identified by an ID and has its extension. So, in practice, a teacher of a department uploads material of a certain format for a lesson of a course that is part of a degree course.
