The data model describes two main entities: `SUBSCRIPTION_PLAN` and `FEATURES`. The `SUBSCRIPTION_PLAN` entity is uniquely identified by its primary key `plan_id`. The `FEATURE` entity is defined by a primary identifier `feature_code` and a `feature_name` attribute. There is a Many-to-Many (N:N) cardinality relationship between the two entities, indicating that a subscription plan can include multiple features and a feature can be included in multiple plans. This relationship is characterized by its own attribute, `monthly_cost`, which qualifies the specific cost of the association between a single feature and a specific subscription plan.
