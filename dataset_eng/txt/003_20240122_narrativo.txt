This system was designed to map and manage the organizational architecture of a dynamic and structured company. At the highest level, the entire organization is spread across several physical locations, each uniquely identified. Each headquarters acts as a container for the various company functions, hosting within it one or more specific departments. Each department, whether dedicated to marketing, research and development or human resources, is unequivocally linked to a single location, thus ensuring a clear geographical and logistical location for each team. Within this structure, staff are organized into distinct roles. The managers guide the strategies and operations of each department; each manager belongs to only one department, thus concentrating their leadership and skills in a well-defined area, although a single department can benefit from the guidance and supervision of multiple management figures. To support daily activities, we find the fundamental figure of assistants. Similar to managers, each assistant is formally assigned to a single department, which represents his or her professional "base" within the company. However, the system is designed to promote maximum flexibility and collaboration: a manager can in fact avail himself of the support of multiple assistants to manage the complexity of his tasks, and, at the same time, a single assistant can serve and collaborate with several managers. This flexible interaction allows you to create synergies and optimize resources, ensuring agile and transversal support where it is most needed.
