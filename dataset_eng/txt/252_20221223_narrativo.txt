This system was designed to tell the story of a university, not only through its structures, but through the people and places that bring it to life. At the center of this universe is the Student, the true protagonist, uniquely identified by his Student Number. For each student we keep essential information such as Surname, Name, Date of Birth and Grade Point Average, which testifies to their academic progress. The educational journey of each student takes place within a specific Degree Course, in turn defined by a Code and a Name, to which numerous other colleagues can be enrolled. Furthermore, each student belongs to a single Department, his academic house, which welcomes a multitude of students. Each Department is a very distinct entity, recognizable by an identification code, a name, the year of its foundation and even a logo color that represents its visual identity. The geographical dimension is fundamental for mapping our community: each Department is based in a specific City, although the same City can host multiple departmental headquarters. The student's personal history is also linked to a place, since each individual is associated with their own city of origin, and naturally multiple students can share the same origin. Each City, characterized by its own Code and Name, is not an isolated entity, but is in turn part of a Nation to which it belongs, also defined by a Code and a Name, thus allowing us to understand the geographical distribution of both our structures and our student population.
