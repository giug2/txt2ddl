So basically here we have some stuff that revolves around music. There's the matter of Festivals, each one is its own thing. Inside every festival, obviously, there are a lot of Performances, that is, performances. The particular thing is that each performance is linked to one and only one Musical Genre, as if the performance *were* of that genre and that's it, and this defines it together with the festival in which it is located. Then, every single performance is connected to a bunch of Songs, so a performance groups together quite a few different songs under itself. Finally, there are the Artists; the strange thing here is that a single artist seems to be connected to a bunch of different musical genres, as if he were responsible for multiple styles. In short, it's a whole thing where festivals have performances, each performance has its own genre and a set of songs, and the artists are somehow associated with various musical genres.
