So basically we have some stuff here about sports clubs. Each club has its own code so as not to confuse them, a name, a color like that of the shirt, and the year in which it was born. 'This company, obviously, has its own headquarters, therefore it is linked to a specific city; multiple clubs can also be in the same city, but a team is only in one place, it's not like it's divided. The cities, in turn, have a name and their own identification code and, as is obvious, each city belongs to a single nation, even if there are a lot of cities in a nation. Then there are the players, who are the most important thing. Each club has quite a few players in its team, but one player, at any given time, plays for only one club. We know the usual things about each player: name, surname, how tall he is, how much he weighs, and here too there is a code to identify him. On top of that, every player was born somewhere, so we connect it to their city of birth, and clearly multiple players can be born in the same place. Finally, each player has his own role on the pitch, such as attacker or defender, and these roles also have their own name and code to clarify. A lot of players can play the same role, but one player has a very specific role.
