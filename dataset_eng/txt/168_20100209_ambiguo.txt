In this system, essentially, we have two big lists of things. On the one hand there are the Projects, and to recognize them from each other we use a Project Code, which is its main identifier. On the other side there is the list of Employees, each with their Serial Number which serves to avoid confusion between them. We also know the surname, name and when they started working for each employee, i.e. their hire date. The interesting thing is how these two lists talk to each other: an employee can be involved in multiple projects at the same time, and, obviously, a project needs a team of several employees. When we join an employee to a project, we must also specify which Role he/she plays in that specific context, information that only makes sense when the two are connected.
