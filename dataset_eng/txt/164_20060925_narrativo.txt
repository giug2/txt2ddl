Welcome to the beating heart of music event organisation, a system designed to create festivals that are memorable in every detail. At the center of this universe is the **Festival**, a unique event identified by its own code, with an evocative name and precise start and end dates that define its time frame. But a festival is nothing without its music. In fact, each event is brought to life through a series of **Performances**, which represent the magical moment in which an **Artist** takes to a **Stage**. Each artist is cataloged not only by an identification code, but also by his musical genre and year of formation, thus offering a clear musical identity. Likewise, each stage has its own name and a maximum capacity of spectators, fundamental elements for managing audience turnout. The relationship is clear: a single performance inextricably links a specific artist to a specific stage within a specific festival.

To enrich the participants' experience and guide them through the vast musical offering, we have introduced the concept of **Thematic Path**. Imagine being able to follow a common thread, like “the icons of rock” or “the new voices of jazz,” that connects different performances into a single coherent narrative. A thematic itinerary can include numerous performances, and the same exhibition can be part of multiple itineraries, creating fascinating plots. To ensure a smooth experience, the system defines a precise order for each stop within each route.

Going beyond simple individual exploration, these thematic itineraries can be experienced through a **Guided Tour**. Each tour is defined by a name, a departure time and a duration, and is closely linked to a single thematic route, acting as an official guide to discover its secrets. To manage participation in these exclusive experiences, each guided tour can be organized on multiple dates. Thus was born the **Tour Group**, which represents the concrete instance of a tour on a specific day, keeping track of the number of members to guarantee everyone a quality experience that is never overcrowded. In this way, from the overall planning of the festival to the management of the individual group of enthusiasts on a guided tour, every aspect of the event is connected to create an unforgettable and perfectly orchestrated musical adventure.
