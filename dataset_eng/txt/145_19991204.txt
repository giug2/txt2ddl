The schema has a more detailed and normalized structure 
The Books entity now defined only by Title Code and Genre with the latter acting as a foreign key towards a new specific Genres relationship described by Genre Sigil and Name 
The relationship between books and writers is managed through an associative table called Authors containing the Book and Writer attributes 
This table creates an explicit link with referential integrity constraints towards Books and Writers allowing a book to have multiple authors and a writer to be associated with multiple books 
Even the more structured management of editions, the Editions Book Publisher Series Year relationship not only connects to Books and Publishers but also to a new Series entity defined by Collation Initials and Name 
The descriptions of the entities Publishers Name City and Writers Code Surname Name remain unchanged compared to the previous scheme
