Welcome to our system, designed to offer you a simple and personalized shopping experience. At the center of everything is you, the user. In order to best serve you, we create a personal profile for you, uniquely identified by your email. In this dedicated space, we store fundamental data such as your name and surname, the date you registered for the first time and, of course, the shipping address to have your products delivered. Every time you decide to make a purchase, our system generates a new order, which is assigned a unique identification code to be able to track it precisely. The structure is designed to inextricably link each purchase to the person who made it: this means that each user can place an unlimited number of orders over time, thus building their own purchase history, while each single order belongs exclusively and securely to a single user, guaranteeing privacy and impeccable management.
