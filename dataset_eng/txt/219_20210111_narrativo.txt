Welcome to the beating heart of our academic management system, a digital universe designed to map and interconnect every aspect of university life. At the center of it all we find the University, each institution clearly identified by a unique acronym and described by its full name. Each university is divided into numerous Departments, which constitute its organizational backbone; each department is defined by its own code, a name and a physical location, and is inextricably linked to its university. The real driving force of the academy are the teachers, unique people registered in the system with a personal identification number, name and surname. Their professional life is rich and multifaceted: a teacher can collaborate with multiple departments at the same time and, for each assignment, the system specifies his exact classification, such as "Full Professor" or "Researcher". In addition to professional life, the system also keeps track of personal information, connecting each teacher to their municipality of birth and their municipality of residence, each identified by a unique Istat code. On the educational offering side, we find the Degree Courses, each with its own identification code and name. These courses do not exist in isolation, but are closely connected to the organizational structure: a department can offer several degree courses and, vice versa, a degree course can be interdisciplinary in nature, thus being managed by multiple departments. To provide a further level of logical aggregation, each degree course belongs to a specific Faculty, a larger entity, identified by a code and a name, which groups together similar study paths. In this way, the system creates a complete and dynamic information network, capable of representing the complex and fascinating reality of the university world, from its institutional foundations to the individuals who animate it and the training courses it offers.
