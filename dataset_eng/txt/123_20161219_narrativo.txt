Welcome to a digital universe designed to explore and catalog the world's artistic heritage. This system allows you to embark on a journey that starts from the nations to reach the heart of a work of art, revealing every detail and connection. Our journey begins on a global map, where each nation is defined by a name and a unique acronym that identifies it. Within each nation, we discover a mosaic of Cities, each with its own name and unique identifier, but belonging unmistakably to just one nation. Cities are the custodians of culture, and in fact each of them can host numerous museums. Each museum, in turn, is a unique entity, recognized by a specific code and its name, and its location is fixed in one and only city. Crossing the threshold of a museum, you enter its rooms: each museum is divided into several rooms, each with its own size and name. The identity of a room is so linked to its museum that to recognize it without ambiguity it is necessary to know both the name and the code of the museum that hosts it. It is within these rooms that the true treasures are found: the works of art. Each room can display multiple masterpieces, but each individual work, with its title, year of execution and a unique catalog code, is placed in one and only one room. Finally, no work would exist without its creator. The system celebrates the genius of the Artists, each registered with an identification code. A single artist can be the author of numerous works of art, but each masterpiece in our catalog is traced back to the talent of a single, specific artist, thus closing the circle that links geography, architecture, art and human genius.
