Imagine a vibrant universe of music and celebrations, a system designed to capture the essence and organization of great musical events. At the center of this world we find the concept of **CircuitoFestival**, a real family of events united under one big name and identified by a unique code. Each circuit, with its identity and style, manifests itself over time through its multiple **Festival Editions**. Each of these editions represents a unique moment, a specific chapter in the history of the circuit, unmistakably defined by the start date and the year in which it takes place. Naturally, each edition comes to life in a specific place, a **City** that hosts it, becoming its pulsating setting; while a city can be the birthplace of numerous different festival editions over the years, each individual edition is inextricably linked to a single location.

The heart of each edition is live music, represented by the individual **Exhibitions**. Each edition of the festival is made up of a rich program of performances, each with its own identifying code, which take place on a specific stage at a pre-established time, marking the rhythm of the entire event. But who are the protagonists of these performances? Here a fascinating network of connections comes into play. Each performance can feature multiple artists, and at the same time, an artist featured on the bill can take the stage for several performances during the festival. This complex interaction is managed through the **ArtistaInCartellone** entity, which represents the specific presence of an artist in the program of an edition.

Going deeper, we discover that each **ArtistaInBillboard** is linked to a specific **Artist**, recognized by his or her stage name and a personal code, allowing us to trace the career of an individual musician through his or her participation in various festivals over time. Additionally, the system recognizes that artists are often part of a larger collective, a **Band**. Each artist featured on the bill is in fact associated with the band they are part of. Finally, to add a further level of depth and context, the system links each **Band** to its **City** of origin, creating a geographical map of musical talent and telling not only who plays, but also where the music that animates our festivals comes from.
