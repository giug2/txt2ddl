The data model defines two main entities: Customer and Current Account. The Customer entity is uniquely identified by the Tax Code (Tax Code), which acts as a primary key, and includes personal details such as Surname, Name, BirthDate and Main Address. The Current Account entity is identified by its Number, which constitutes its primary key. There is a many-to-many (N:N) cardinality relationship between the two entities, which means that a customer can be the owner of multiple accounts and, vice versa, an account can be jointly held by multiple customers. This association has its own descriptive attribute, "Specific address", which qualifies the link between a specific customer and a specific account, allowing you to register an address valid exclusively in the context of that specific relationship.
