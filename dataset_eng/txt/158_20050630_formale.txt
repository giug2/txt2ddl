The data model describes a management system for vehicle rental. The central entity is `Vehicle`, identified by the primary key `pk_veicolo` and characterized by attributes such as `model`, `license plate`, `segment`, `Number of Seats`, `registration_year` and `engine registration`. Each vehicle is associated with a single `Branch` entity in a one-to-many relationship; the `Branch` is defined by `pk_branch` (PK) and `city`. The model predicts many-to-many relationships between `Vehicle` and the entities `Equipment Type` and `Promotion Type`, indicating that a vehicle can have multiple trims and be eligible for different promotions. Both `Staging Type` (with PK `pk_type_setting`) and `TipoPromozione` (with PK `pk_tipo_promozione`) have a `description` attribute. Tariff management is modeled through the `TariffaNoleggio` entity, identified by `pk_tariffa_noleggio` and containing the `daily_amount`. This entity is the point of convergence of three one-to-many relationships. Specifically, a `Rental Rate` is associated with a single `Vehicle`, a `Period` (identified by `pk_period` and `name`) and an `Additional Service` (identified by `pk_additional_service` and `name`). As a result, a vehicle, period or additional service may have multiple distinct rental rates associated with it.
