This system was designed to manage a detailed and well-organized product catalog, putting the user experience and the value of their feedback at the center. The beating heart of our universe is the **Product**, each item is unique and recognizable thanks to its **SKU** identification code, accompanied by a **Name** that describes its essence. To enrich knowledge of each product, we give customers a voice through the **Reviews** system. Every single review is a valuable contribution, identified by its own unique **ReviewID**, and contains the **Date** of publication and a **Rating** that expresses the degree of satisfaction. The relationship is clear: a product can collect an unlimited number of reviews over time, but each review is inextricably linked to a single and specific product, providing a faithful picture of its perception.

To ensure that customers can easily navigate and find what they are looking for, products are placed in a logical and intuitive hierarchical structure. At the top of this organizational pyramid we find the **Category**, a broad and general grouping identified by a unique **CategoryID** and a **Name**. Each **Category**, in turn, is divided into one or more more specific **Subcategories**, which allow you to refine your search. A **Subcategory** is uniquely defined by the combination of its **SubcategoryCod** and the identifier of the parent category with which it is associated (**fk_category**), and is further enriched by a **Name** and a **Description** to guide the user. This creates a strong link: a single category can contain numerous subcategories, but each subcategory unambiguously belongs to only one parent category. Finally, the system connects each element: each **Product** finds its precise location within a specific **Subcategory**. This way, a subcategory can accommodate a wide variety of items, but each product is categorized in one place, ensuring a clean, consistent, and easy-to-explore catalog.
