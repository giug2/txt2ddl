The scheme outlines an information system in which there are Nations, each identified by a Nation Abbreviation and described by a Name 
The Manufacturers defined by an identification code, a name and the country to which they belong are connected to these, establishing a referential integrity bond with the Nations entity. 
However, a new Category entity identified by a Category Code and a Name is introduced. Consequently, the Models entity, while maintaining its identification key composed of House and Name, now establishes a referential integrity constraint with the new Categories entity through its Category attribute 
The most significant change is the inclusion of the Versions entity which is interposed between the models and the cars 
Each Version identified by the trio of attributes House Model and Version Code has a Displacement attribute and refers to the Models entity via the House and Model pair 
The Cars entity always identified by the License Plate and characterized by the Year is no longer linked directly to a model but to a specific Version through a referential constraint on the House, Model and Version attributes  
Finally, the ownership relationship is made explicit through a new associative entity Property that connects a Car through the Car attribute to an Owner through the Owner attribute, establishing referential integrity constraints with the respective Cars and People entities
The latter collects the personal information of individuals identified by their Tax Code and described by Surname and Name
