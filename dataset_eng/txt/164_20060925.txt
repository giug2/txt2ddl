Show the conceptual scheme of a database for the management of music festivals, according to the following specifications.
• The festival area has a series of stages, each of which has a name and a maximum capacity of spectators.
• A festival has a name, a start date and an end date.
• Different artists perform in each festival and the same artist can participate in multiple festivals.
• For each artist, the musical genre, the year of formation, and the stage on which they perform are represented (which can change at different festivals).
It is of interest to represent possible thematic itineraries of a festival, each with a code and consisting of an ordered list of stages to visit and, for each stage, an ordered list of artists to listen to.
• We also want to represent guided tours of the festival which each have a name, a starting time, a duration and are based on a certain thematic route. The guided tours are repeated on the various days of the festival with a different number of participants.
