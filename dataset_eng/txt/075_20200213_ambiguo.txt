So, basically here we have all the organization of a university, more or less. There is the stuff of Schools, which are the largest entities, each with its own identification code and name. Under each School, then, you will find a lot of Departments; each department obviously belongs to a single school and also has its own code and a name to recognize it. Going into more detail, each department manages different Study Courses, and each course, which has its own name and ID, is necessarily linked to a single department. Finally there are the Professors. For each of them we write down a name and a code, but the interesting thing is how they are linked to the courses. This is not a simple thing, because one professor can teach multiple courses of study and, at the same time, a single course can have quite a few different professors working on it. In practice, to know who teaches where, you really have to look at the specific pairing between a professor and a course.
