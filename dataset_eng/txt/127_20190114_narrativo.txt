This system was designed to map and chronicle academia, creating a rich tapestry of information that connects institutions, places and people. At the center of this universe lies the entity of the University, each university is uniquely identified by its own Code and described by its official name and its date of foundation, which testifies to its history. Naturally, every university is rooted in a precise geographical context; for this reason, each institute is closely associated with a single city. In turn, each City is defined by an identification code, its name and the country it belongs to, and can act as the cradle for numerous universities, creating lively cultural hubs. But the true beating heart of every university is the people who animate it. Our system registers each Professor, assigning him a personal code and recording his surname, name and date of birth. The connection between teachers and institutions is fundamental: while a university draws on the knowledge and experience of many professors, every single professor is affiliated with one and only one university. Finally, to define the role and career of each teacher, the Qualification comes into play. Each qualification, such as "Ordinary" or "Associate", is identified by a Code and a specific Name. This allows us to establish that each professor holds a specific qualification, while, naturally, the same qualification can be shared by numerous professors within the academic panorama. Together, these elements draw a detailed and interconnected map, capable of navigating between the cities of knowledge, exploring their universities and getting to know the teachers who define their excellence.
