So basically we have this system that handles a bunch of university stuff. There are Departments, each with its own name and an identification code that is unique to it. Each department, then, has a lot of degree courses under it. These courses also have a name, a code to recognize them and we also know where they are held. Naturally, a graduate program is full of students. Each student is identified by a unique student number, as well as having his or her name, and is obviously enrolled in only one degree course. Finally, there is the matter of exams. Each student can take quite a few exams, and for each exam they take, they record the date and grade. Each exam recording has its own reporting code, but to be sure which exam we are talking about, you need to look at both that code and the student number of the student who took it.
