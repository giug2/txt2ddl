The data model defines the structure for managing warehouses, products and their geographical location. The `PROVINCE` entity is identified by the primary key `PROVINCE_ABSCRIPTION` and contains the `PROVINCE_NAME`. The `MUNICIPALITY` entity, identified by the `ISTAT_CODE`, has the `MUNICIPAL_NAME` attribute and is linked to a single province via an N:1 relationship. In turn, the `WAREHOUSE` entity is identified by `WAREHOUSE_ID` and described by `LOCATION_NAME` and `YEAR_OPENING`; each warehouse is located in a single municipality, establishing an N:1 relationship. For products, the `CATEGORY` entity is defined by `CATEGORY_ID` (PK) and `CATEGORY_NAME`. The `PRODUCT` entity is uniquely identified by the `SKU` and includes the `PRODUCT_NAME`, `BRAND` and `LAUNCH_DATE` attributes. Each product belongs to a single category, according to an N:1 cardinality relationship. Finally, the model establishes a one-to-many (1:N) relationship between `WAREHOUSE` and `PRODUCT`, implying that a warehouse can contain multiple products and each product is associated with a single, specific warehouse.
