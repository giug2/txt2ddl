The scheme describes a logistics management system organized around four main entities.
The first is the Warehouse, uniquely identified by its Warehouse Code and characterized by an Address.
The Employee is connected to this entity, who is identified by a unique Serial Number and described by a Name.
Each Employee is assigned to a specific Warehouse, thus establishing a one-to-many relationship.
Goods are represented by two related entities: Product and Lot.
The Product entity defines the general characteristics of an item, uniquely associating a SKU with its ProductName.
The Lot entity, however, represents a specific production batch of that item; each lot has its own Lot Code, is characterized by a Production Date, refers to a single Product and is managed by a single Personnel, thus creating a direct link with the other entities.
