The Degree Courses and Students entities remain conceptually identical to the first scheme. However, the Departments are connected to a new Campus entity that associates a CampusCode with an extended CampusName. The Teachings entity is also introduced to further detail the degree courses; each course, linked to a Department-Degree Course pair, is identified by its own Teaching Code and has a specific number of ECTS. Consequently, the SessioniEsame entity, always identified by a SessionID and with an Academic Year, is no longer directly connected to the degree course but to one of its specific courses through a composite reference to Department, Degree Course and Course. Finally, the participation relationship is modeled more explicitly through the Examination Registration entity, which connects an Examination Session via reference to the Examination Sessions entity to a Student with reference to the Students entity, also recording the Registration Date.
