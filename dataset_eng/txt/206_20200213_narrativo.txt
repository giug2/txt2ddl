This system was designed to map and manage the entire organizational architecture of a company, connecting people, their workplaces and the activities they carry out. At the heart of this structure we find the Divisions, which represent the great pillars on which the organization is based; each division is a unique and fundamental entity. Within this framework, Projects come to life, which are the initiatives and tasks carried out by the company. It is important to note that a project cannot exist in isolation, but is always intrinsically linked to one and only one Division, which has full responsibility and supervision. At the same time, the organization has various offices, conceived as distinct operational units or physical locations. The true strength of the system emerges when we introduce the figure of the Employee. Each person who works in the company is identified not only by his unique code, but also by the context in which he operates. In fact, to precisely define an employee, it is essential to associate him with a specific Division to which he belongs and a single Office in which he carries out his duties. This structure ensures that each employee is clearly placed within the organizational chart, belonging to a single division and working from a single office, thus creating a clear, orderly and unambiguous organizational model.
