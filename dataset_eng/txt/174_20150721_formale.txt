The data model describes the management of student careers and related administrative transactions within an academic institution. The Department entity, uniquely identified by the Department Code (PK), represents the basic organizational structure and has a Department Name. Each Department can offer one or more Degree Courses; the CorsoDiLaurea entity, identified by the CourseCode (PK) and described by the CourseName, is therefore in a one-to-many relationship with the relevant Department. The Student entity contains personal information, with the Student Number as the primary key (PK) and the Surname, Name and Date of Birth attributes. The relationship between a Student and a Degree Course is formalized through the Course Registration entity, which represents the registration act for a specific Academic Year and is identified by an Registration_ID (PK). A Student can have multiple enrollments over time and a Degree Course can accommodate multiple enrollments. The related financial transactions are linked to each Course Registration: the Tax entity, identified by Tax_ID (PK), records the Amount and IssueDate of each charge due, while the Payment entity, with primary key ID_Pagamento, tracks the Amount and PaymentDate of each payment made. Each membership may generate multiple fees and may be paid through multiple payments.
