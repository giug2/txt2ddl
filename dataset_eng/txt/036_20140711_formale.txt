The data schema defines four entities: Department, Professor, Edition and Course. The Department entity is uniquely identified by the 'Name' (PK) attribute and contains the 'Address' information. The Professor entity is identified by a 'Code' (PK) and includes the 'Name' attribute. The Course entity is defined by a 'Code' (PK) and a 'Title'. The Edition entity, identified by a 'Code' (PK), has a 'Year' attribute. The relationships are structured as follows: there is a one-to-many relationship between Department and Professor, indicating that each professor belongs to a single department. The Edition entity is the point of convergence of two distinct one-to-many relationships: one with the Professor entity, where a professor can hold multiple editions but each edition has only one teacher, and one with the Course entity, where a course can have multiple editions but each edition refers to a single course.
