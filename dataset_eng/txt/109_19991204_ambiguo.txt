So basically we're keeping track of a few book related things here. There is a list of writers, where each one is identified by their own unique code, and then obviously we have their first and last name. Then there are the books, also with a code that distinguishes them from each other, the title and the genre, in short, to understand what type of book it is. The interesting thing is how writers and books are linked: an author can write a lot of different books, but it can also happen that a book is written by several people, so it's a bit of a cross-connection. Finally, there are the publishers, who are identified by their name, which cannot be the same for two different publishers, and we also know in which city they are located. Each book is linked to only one publisher, it cannot be published by more than one, but a publisher, obviously, publishes many books. When we put together a book and its publisher, we also note two other things, that is, which series it was published in and the year of publication.
