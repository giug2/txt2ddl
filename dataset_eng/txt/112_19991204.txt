The scheme outlines an information system in which there are Nations, each identified by a Nation Abbreviation and described by a Name 
The Manufacturers defined by an identification code, a name and the country to which they belong are connected to these, establishing a referential integrity bond with the Nations entity. 
Each manufacturer in turn produces Models which are identified by the pair of attributes Home and Name and characterized by a Category. The Home attribute creates a referential link with the respective Manufacturer 
The individual cars are registered using a unique license plate and can be traced back to a specific model through the House and Model attributes which together form a reference to the Models entity 
Each car is also described by a Year of production and associated with an Owner whose code refers to the People entity 
The latter collects the personal information of individuals identified by their Tax Code and described by Surname and Name
