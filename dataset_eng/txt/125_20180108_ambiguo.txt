So basically we have some stuff on sports clubs. Each company has its own code to recognize it, a name, a color and a date of foundation. These companies, obviously, are not in the air, but are located in a very specific city; in short, a city can host a lot of different companies, but a company only has its headquarters in one city. Speaking of cities, they also have an identification code and a name, and each city is part of a nation, which in turn is identified by a code and a name. So, within a country you find a bunch of cities. Returning to the clubs, they are full of players. Each player is linked to only one club, but a club has many. For each player we know a personal code, the name and surname, and then the usual things like height and weight. Finally, each player has a specific role, such as attacker or defender. There is a list of roles, each with its own code and name, and a role can be filled by many different players.
