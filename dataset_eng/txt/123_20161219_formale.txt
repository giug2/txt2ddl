The data model describes a management system for museums and works of art. The NATION entity, uniquely identified by an ABBREVIATION, is associated with multiple CITY entities through a one-to-many relationship; each CITY, identified by an ID, belongs to only one nation. In turn, a CITY can host multiple MUSEUMS, each identified by a CODE, while a museum is located in only one city. The ROOM entity is defined by a primary key composed of its NAME and a reference to the MUSEUM it belongs to (fk_museo), indicating that each room belongs to a single museum, which can contain multiple ones. The ARTIST entity is identified by a unique CODE. Finally, the WORK OF ART entity, identified by a CODE and described by attributes such as TITLE and YEAR OF EXECUTION, is connected both to a single ARTIST who creates it and to a single ROOM in which it is exhibited, establishing in both cases a one-to-many relationship.
