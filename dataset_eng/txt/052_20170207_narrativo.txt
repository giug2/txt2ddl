This system is designed to map the operational ecosystem of a dynamic company, illustrating how people, customers and businesses intertwine to achieve goals. At the center of everything are our Customers, each registered with a unique identifier and name. The relationship with a customer is the starting point for every initiative; in fact, a single Client can commission multiple Projects over time, creating a continuous flow of work and collaboration. Each Project, in turn, is identified by its own code and name, but is always linked exclusively to a single Client, ensuring that every effort is targeted and responds to specific needs. To carry out these projects, the company relies on its internal structure, organized into Departments, such as "Research and Development" or "Marketing", each with its own identifier and name. It is within these Departments that we find the beating heart of the company: the Employees. Each Employee, identified by a serial number and a name, belongs to a single Department, creating a clear hierarchical and membership structure, while a Department, naturally, welcomes numerous professionals within it. The crucial moment is the assignment of work: to bring a project to life, a team composed of several employees is formed. This model ensures that a group of people work together towards a common goal. To ensure maximum concentration and dedication, each Employee is assigned to work on a single Project at a time, dedicating all their energies to the success of that specific initiative.
