This system was designed to map and manage the entire organizational structure of a company, creating a clear and hierarchical framework that starts from the top and reaches every single individual. At the heart of this model we find the main entity, the Company, uniquely identified by its own code, the company_id. It represents the supreme container, the root from which the entire organization branches out. To function efficiently, each Company is divided into multiple operating units called Divisions. A Division cannot exist alone; its very identity is defined not only by its specific code, the division_id, but also by the indissoluble bond with its parent company, represented by the id of the company it belongs to. This means that while a Company may have numerous Divisions, each Division reports to and belongs to one and only one Company. Going into further detail, each Division is in turn organized into smaller and more specialized work groups, the Teams. Similar to the previous logic, a Team is identified by a combination of its team_id and the id of the Division in which it operates. This structure ensures that each Team is clearly located within a specific Division, allowing a single Division to coordinate the activities of many Teams. Finally, the fundamental piece of this organization is the Employee. Each Employee is an active member of a Team and his position is uniquely defined by his employee_id and the id of the Team he is part of. This chain of membership ensures that a Team can be made up of multiple Employees, but each Employee is assigned to one and only one Team, thus creating a clear line of responsibility and collaboration within the company structure.
