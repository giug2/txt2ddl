Imagine a digital culinary universe where every creation, from the simplest appetizer to the most elaborate dessert, has its own story and its own place of honor. At the center of this world are the **Chefs**, true taste artists, each uniquely identified. Each **Chef** can create an infinite number of **Dishes**, inextricably linking his name to each of his creations. These culinary works are not isolated, but are expertly organized within specific **Menu**. Each **Menu** is conceived as a unique gastronomic journey and can include a variety of different **Dishes**, designed to harmonize with each other.

To give order and structure to each experience, each **Dish** is classified according to a precise **Dish Type**. This categorization, which can range from starters to first courses, from second courses to desserts, allows you to define the role of each course within the **Menu**. A single typology, such as "first course", can naturally be associated with countless different dishes in various menus. But what's behind each flavor? The **Recipe**. The system also archives the secret heart of each preparation, which contains the instructions. It is interesting to note how **Recipes** are also linked to their category: in fact, a single **Dish Type**, for example "dessert", can group together a vast repertoire of different recipes, offering an archive of creative possibilities. In summary, this system models a complete gastronomic ecosystem, where chefs create dishes, dishes make up menus, and each course is defined by a typology that connects it not only to its role in the meal, but also to a set of recipes that represent its essence.
