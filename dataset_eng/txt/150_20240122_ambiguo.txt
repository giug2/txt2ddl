So basically here we have a few things that revolve around a university or something like that. There are the professors, each with their own code to recognize him, and then there are the departments, also with their own identifier. It works like this: a group of professors reports to a single department, so each department has its own team of teachers. Then there are the students, also with a sort of matriculation number, and a lot of students are connected to a single department, a bit like professors. Above all there are the campuses, which have their own ID, and each campus can have different departments inside. The messiest part is the connection between professors and students: a professor has to deal with many students, and at the same time a student can take lessons from a lot of different professors, so there is a big mix between them.
